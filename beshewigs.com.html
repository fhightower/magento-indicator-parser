Wigs in an Online Store – Really?

When ordering wigs from an online store, consider the type of material you want your wig to be made from, the style, cut and color, and then browse around and look at wigs to find the ones you like.

To order wigs from an online store, you will need to take your head measurements, including around your head form the hairline on your forehead, above your ears, to the nape of your neck; over the top of your head from the hairline in the front to the base in the back; and across your head from ear to ear.

Finally, you will need to decide on the cap construction you want your wigs to have. There are a number of different cap constructions, including traditional weft, open cap wefts, lace front, and hand-stitched monofilament. Each of these offers a different level of quality and price.

Once you have your measurements and have chosen a style, color, and type of hair, and determine the cap construction, it is easy to order wigs from an online store.
