GotStock Hardware Online - Building & Industrial Supplies


GotStock helps Australian businesses save time and money on building supplies, industrial supplies and hardware online. We make it easy for businesses to manage their ongoing consumable needs with our online solution. We're part of the Wesfarmers Industrial & Safety division, and thanks to our large buying power we can pass on big cost savings on a range of building & industrial supplies across Abrasives, Greases & Lubrication, Tools, Workwear, Lifting, Cleaning, Catering and more.


Read more...

When it comes to the building supplies industry, going to the hardware store can be a time-consuming, cumbersome task that leads to stuttering productivity. Leaving a job site to drive all the way to the store to wander the large aisles and tracking down people to grab building supplies off shelves that stretch up toward the ceiling can quickly become frustrating. That frustration is only multiplied when it comes to buying mass quantities of items for a business, where costs can quickly stack up. Deadlines for jobs are already tight, and wasting time going to and from the hardware store only adds to the pressure and takes away from the bottom line.


Thats where GotStock comes in. GotStock houses all the industrial supplies you need and is the easiest place to buy hardware online. Now instead of wasting time making runs to suppliers, the supplier comes to you, whether at home, at the office or on location at a job. Part of the tried and trusted Wesfarmers Industrial & Safety entity, GotStock is able to buy in mass quantities at lower costs. Those savings are then passed on to customers, making the site not only convenient, but inexpensive too.


GotStock eliminates some of the dangers of ordering industrial supplies online by offering fast delivery Australia wide. Products from a range of top manufacturers with warranties intact that are always in stock put your mind at ease. No more worrying about wasting time going all the way to the hardware store only to find out a key item is out of stock, or sweating on whether or not an online order is going to come in on time.


Another thing that makes GotStock so convenient is the sheer amount of building supplies in its inventory. Its a one-stop shop for everything in the industrial world. Fasteners, electrical products, abrasives, adhesives and fittings are just some of the things at your fingertips. Even larger products such as ladders and pallet trucks are just a click away. You can also outfit employees with high visibility clothing, hard hats, footwear and other safety gear or high-quality items such as tool belts through GotStock.


Its never been easier to get a hold of bulk quantities of building supplies. Whats even better is the more you buy, the more you save. Stocking up is the cheapest way to go and keeps you prepared to take care of business in the future. Whether youre looking to fill a truck, garage or warehouse, GotStock is the place to replenish your dwindling building supplies.


Time-wasting, expensive trips to the hardware store are now a thing of the past with GotStock. Its never been easier to quickly find whatever industrial supplies you need. Whether youre on a computer at your desk back at base or on a phone or tablet at a job site, shopping for hardware online saves you time and money. Thousands of industrial and hardware supplies at your fingertips delivered on site in one business day at a low cost? Thats GotStock.
