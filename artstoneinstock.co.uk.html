Artstone in Stock

Cast stone, the cost effective alternative to natural stone.

Artstone in Stock has the capacity to meet the needs of local building companies, builders merchants and national house contractors. We stock a large selection of products at reasonable prices. These stock items eliminate the lengthy lead times often given by large manufacturers whilst they produce bespoke items.

We stock heads (lintels) cills, copings, pier caps, spheres, string/ plinth, keystones, corbels, ashlar, quoins, gable features and balustrade. Please shop around our site or feel free to call us to discuss your requirements

We mainly stock semi-dry products but from time to time we do take stock of GRC (quoins and occasionally cills) and Wet-cast (string and copings) all these products can be found in the clearance section of the website.


Products are usually stocked in a standard buff colour. a swatch can be ordered via the colour samples tab above. We can source products in alternative colours should you require. All items shown on the website are in stock but should you order large quantities it may be advisable to call/ email to check we have everything you require as stock levels do fluctuate. We can advise of the lead time should this circumstance arise.

We pride ourselves on quick dispatch and guarantee delivery of goods within 7 working days if not sooner!


We work closely with select manufacturers to ensure quality goods. Goods are stocked on pallets and shrink wrapped for delivery. Goods are brought to you on rigid lorries with a tail lift for offloading purposes.

Technical documentation, lifting and handling advice and FAQ's are available to view on our website, however if you cannot find the answer you are looking for please do contact the office.

Purchasing your artstone with us is simple and payment can be made through PayPal should you require to make payment in another form please contact orders@artstoneinstock.co.uk . We will shortly be able to card payments over the phone.
