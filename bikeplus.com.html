About Bikeplus

Bikeplus carries the Avanti and BMC brands, with a wide range of models that include Road, TT, MTB and Hybrid bikes.

Bikeplus also carries a full range of components, accessories, apparel and nutrition. We have a full service centre, trained mechanics and bike fitting studio.
