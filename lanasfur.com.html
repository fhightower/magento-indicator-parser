SOLUTIONS

  • Clean Fur
    To maintain natural fur shine and prolong life of fur, your fur items need to be cleaned at leasttwice a year. Fur cleaning allows the removal of dust particles, pollutants, and cosmetics that accumulate in fur hairs over time. Such debris greatly reduces the life of fur by drawing natural oils out of the pelt. Lana's has everything you need to master the process of fur cleaning at home, and to avoid the hassle of bringing your precious fur garments to a furrier or cleaning expert. All Lana's products come with detailed simple instructions explaining our cleaning process and methods. Using Lana’s will assure an extended life and beauty of your furs. Show your fur the love it deserves!
  • W
    Whiten Fur
    It's not unusual for old white fur to become yellow with age, especially around collars and sleeves that usually age faster from wear. At Lana’s, we offer a professional whitening solution for at home treatment of aged fur. Now you can whiten your fur at home by using Lana's whitener that is capable of making your fur a few shades whiter with every treatment. Our solution works great and have already been proven safe and efficient.
  • S
    Remove Stains
    Stains is unavoidable annoyance of life. But stains on fur are not just unpleasant, they may shorten the life and shine of expensive coats if not treated in a short period of time. At Lana's, we designed an instant fur spot cleaner in a conveniently sized small spray bottle that can be easily carried in a purse or pocket. Our easy-to-use solution allows you to quickly dissolve and remove tough makeup, lipstick spots, wine, and many other types of stains out of your precious fur garments.
  • Deodorize Fur
    It is very common for cigarette smoke, perfume, old age, pets or must odors to deposit and accumulate in furs. When smell is so strong that your fur retains it even after hanging outside, it is time to take care of it. Instead of spending money on dry cleaning services by expensive professionals, you can deodorize your fur at home quickly and easily using Lana's Fur Odor Remover solution. After the application of Lana's solution the smell will go away instantly. You can also get rid of unpleasant odors by using our Deep Fur Cleaning Kit that additionally provides thorough fur cleaning.
  • R
    Restore Fur
    Give your dry leather what it needs, rehydrate and recondition your old dry fur and make it fabulous again with Lana's Dry Fur Therapy solution! Specifically formulated to rehydrate dry fur clothing, Lana's home remedy helps you restore and prolong life of old furs by strengthening skin and individual hair follicles on fur pelts. By softening leather side of fur with few easy steps, the Lana's Dry Therapy solution lets you protect your fur from shedding.
  • L
    Clean Leather
    Clean and protect your valuable leather items by using Lana's leather conditioner. Our leather care solution helps you to keep your leather possessions clean, preserve the leather, and restore dry and faded leather that was neglected for some time. Our solution has a compact convenient package that was specifically designed for quick and easy application on any type of surface. Lana's unique roll-on bottle design makes it extremely easy to spread the solution evenly and to avoid messy hands.
