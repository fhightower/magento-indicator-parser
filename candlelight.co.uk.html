Introduction

Candlelight is a market leading designer, importer and wholesale supplier of design-led giftware and home accessories to fit every room and every lifestyle.

Candlelight operate in the UK out of our “state of the art” 175,000 sq. ft warehouse, including 10,000sq.ft of showroom packed with innovation and tailored to the needs of both multiples and independent retailers alike.

With over 40 years of knowledge and expertise, Candlelight are able to meet the demands of the large multiple retailer, holding stock and efficiently handling repeat sales, regularly processing orders in excess of £1 million. Candlelight provide a complete and bespoke service from concept and design to sourcing and shipment, with the full support of our design team and product developers.
