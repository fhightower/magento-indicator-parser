Return Policy 

At Mr. Mulch, you may return the new, unused, resalable PACKAGED purchased on our website within 30 days of purchase, some restocking/return fees apply.  Please see the individual item on the website for the fees if they apply.  To ensure top quality bulk products, we do NOT allow the return of any bulk products.  Contact our customer service by filling out the INFORMATION REQUEST FORM to get a return authorization number before bringing any item(s) back. Notifying customer service of the items to be returned will significantly expedite your refund. Once we have provided a return authorization number, simply bring in the item(s) to the return address provided.  If you would like us to pick up the product to return, we can do that for you as well.  There will be an additional fee to pick up the product.  

 

Upon receipt of returned merchandise, we will credit the amount you paid for the product(s), minus our original shipping cost.  We will advise you of the amount of this shipping cost when the return authorization is provided. When returning or exchanging item(s) please keep the original packing. We are unable to send out a box or packaging for returns or exchanges. If you do not have the original packaging, you will be responsible for obtaining a new packaging and/or box.

 

Please note that item(s) will be inspected upon return to determine if the item(s) are in resalable condition. If upon inspection, the item(s) are deemed unsalable, your credit will be subject to approval.

 

Special Order Cancellation Policy

Special orders are orders containing merchandise that is not held in regular stock and is ordered at the specific request of the customer. As such, special orders cannot be cancelled, returned or exchanged under any circumstances once Outdoor Living by Mr. Mulch has placed the order with the manufacturer or after 72 hours when the order is placed. Customer is required to pay the balance of the purchase price when order is placed or under special circumstances when special order items have arrived at our warehouse.  Once balance is paid in full we will deliver or ship the special order items or you can pick them up at our warehouse.  Customer's failure to pay full balance so shall not relieve Customer of its obliigation to pay the entire amount owned for such merchandise. 

 

Delivery Policy 

 

Mr. Mulch Landscape Supply delivers PALLETIZED products such as bagged/packaged mulch, bagged/packaged topsoil, bagged/packaged sand, bagged concrete mix, bagged/packaged gravel, grass seed, straw, sod, Eco-Bricks, bagged/packaged salt, bagged wood pellets or packaged firewood with our flatbed truck within 100 miles of our location in Columbus, Ohio.  We also deliver BULK products such as loose mulch, topsoil, sand, gravel, and firewood with our dump trucks.

 

Our flatbed trucks generally deliver the PALLETIZED or PACKAGED products to the driveway with a forklift type truck, it can place product in a different location on your property.  Keep in mind that you must be present if you want our forklift to place the product in a different location.  We do strive very hard to place the palletized product where you would like it in one location, but this is a very large forklift and it has its limitations.  IT IS ULTIMATELY UP TO THE DRIVER'S DISCRESSION IF HE CAN PLACE THE MATERIAL OFF THE DRIVEWAY.  If the driver damages any part of your property, it is not our responsibility.  This includes but not limited to ruts in yard, damage to trees, damage to driveway on edges when we go off, etc.

 

Our dump trucks generally deliver the BULK products and are generally not the large commercial trucks that bring huge loads (like our flatbed truck), they are residential delivery trucks.  They are still larger than a pick-up truck so keep that in mind when ordering.  Our policy is to delivery the bulk product on the driveway, we strongly discourage our drivers to go off the driveway.  With that being said, there are some situations that we can do this.  It is generally in mid-summer when the ground is very dry.  Please assume that the bulk product will be delivered on your driveway where there are no overhead obstructions.

 

Due to the nature of the packaging and trucks we use, we cannot deliver this packaged or palletized products with any bulk mulch, bulk topsoil, bulk sand, bulk gravel, bulk salt, or bulk firewood.

About

Founded in 1995 by Craig Schweitzer, Mr. Mulch Landscape Supply is the premier landscape supply in central Ohio. The roots of the organization began simply with a few seasonal products like mulch and topsoil. By the late 1990s, the product offering grew to natural stone, concrete pavers & segmented retaining walls, and pond products. The company moved to its current location at 6469 Nicholas Drive in 1999 and built the signature Outdoor Living log cabin store that is now the Outdoor Living by Mr.Mulch..


Our company is committed to the relentless pursuit of improvement. We are never satisfied with our level of service or efficiency; we focus on continuous improvement in everything we do. We are the leader in the Columbus market in the landscape supply industry.

We are also committed to our community by actively supporting various organizations, churches and schools. Since the 1990s, we have helped organizations net over $1,000,000.00 for their causes. We have donated generously with both products and dollars to various groups as well. We are members of the community and proudly serve our community when called upon.
