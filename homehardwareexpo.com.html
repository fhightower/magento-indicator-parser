Home Hardware News

Amerock has been defining cabinet hardware fashion in homes for more than 80 years. Amerock continues to introduce new designs, colors and finishes, based on constant surveys of decorating trends and consumer preferences. Amerock brings consumers fresh, lasting looks which appeal to a variety of tastes. There are thousands of cabinet hardware knob and pull combinations to choose from. Home Hardware Expo is a factory direct distributor for Amerock.

Schlage has produced quality door hardware for over 85 years, earning the trust of both homeowners and contractors with affordable and durable security products. Schlage supplies door hardware for every need throughout your home, including both interior and exterior pieces. The twenty different choices for front-door handlesets, each with an interior lever or knob to match, are just one example of Schlage's many offerings. We've been a distributor for Schlage door hardware since 1988, and we're pleased to offer the full line of Schlage products.

Emtek door hardware offers customers a wide range of options to meet every home need. The solid brass construction of each piece allows strong function to support the beauty of Emtek's hardware designs. Add the collection of secure deadbolts and electronic locks that Emtek now carries, and you have an entire line of door hardware to accompany any home design. Customers have the freedom to specify their hardware choices in great detail thanks to Emtek's practice of individually assembling each order.

The Baldwin name stands out as a trusted leader in door hardware. Combining solid brass composition with a generous assortment of beautiful designs and finishes, Baldwin hardware brings style to any home. Whether rustic, contemporary or something in between, there's a choice for every taste. One of the earliest innovators of door hardware now brings security into the modern era with Baldwin's keyless locks. The convenience of personal entry codes eliminates the need to carry keys yet provides safe, secure protection for your home.
