About Tea Flower

Tea flower is tea ball which is hand woven of white green needle tea that surrounds colourful artistic flowers that bloom when added to hot water. The blooming tea flowers within the green needle tea comprise of Lilies, Jasmine, Marigold and Globe Amaranth which contain all the majestic healing properties the body needs. Their flavours and aromas vary and enhance the experience through colour and beauty.

Unlike ordinary tea this is a light and delicate tea-flower filled with fresh fragrance and lingering sweetness which appeals not only to your sense of taste and sense of smell but also to your sense of sight.
