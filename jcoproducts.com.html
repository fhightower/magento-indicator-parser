Did you know that digital signage and displays increase product visibility and awareness by up to 10 times over conventional non-digital media? Recent studies have shown that digital displays can even improve brand recognition by 40% among consumers, and boost sales by up to 300% when targeted messages are displayed to relevant customers.

Let us introduce you to JCO Digital Media...green, affordable and attractive signage solutions!

At JCO Digital Media, we offer personalized digital signage to meet all of your promotional needs.  Whether you are looking to educate and entertain clients in your waiting rooms, or entice patrons with mouthwatering digital menu boards, we are committed to helping you implement the most effective digital marketing system.

 

We sell and rent a wide variety of environmentally friendly, out-of-home digital displays.  Our in-house graphic design team can even create the perfect customized content that is attractive, dynamic and impactful.  On our website you will find more information about the most common displays that we carry. Our specialty items include commercial-grade digital badges (only 2.4” in diagonal!) and screens up to 19”. These self-contained displays operate like a computer without the need of a computer, software or a costly subscription fee.  All you need to do is load the digital content onto a memory card.  We also carry Digital Signage controllers to convert any home television set into a powerful network that can be operated as standalone or managed remotely.

Make an outstanding first impression. There’s no better way to stand out in a crowd than with our novelty item, the VEO Digital Badge. 

We are proud to be the only company in Canada offering this “punchy” and dynamic tag.  It’s perfect for increasing your exposure at conventions, tourism offices, restaurants or any type of networking event.  With a guaranteed battery life of 12 hours, it will last you the entire day!

Do you require custom signage, beyond our line of inventory? We’re more than happy to help you!

We manufacture LCD displays up to 65”, with customized branding or open-frame for OEMs, as well as a variety of features, including  touch screen, wireless and 3G.
