About American Candle

Welcome to the new American Candle website, the Pocono Mountains ultimate shopping destination. At American Candle you will find all of your favorite brands including one the Poconos largest selections of Vera Bradley, Pandora, Dansko, Swarovski, Jim Shore, Dept. 56., and so much more!

And now you can shop our new online store that we will be updating daily, so make sure to check back often. While you are in the Pocono Mountains, American Candle is a must shop, because there is something for everyone! Our large selection of bulk candy and great gifts make American Candle the clear choice for family fun in the Pocono Mountains.

We hope to see you in the store!

Where to Find Us

American Candle is conveniently located on Route 611 in Bartonsville PA, close to Camelback, Camelbeach and The Crossings Factory Outlets.
