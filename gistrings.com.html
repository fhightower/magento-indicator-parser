Welcome to the home of The Gi String Company

Don't you just hate those flat cotton type gi strings that come as standard in most Brazilian Jiu Jitsu gi pants?

 

The way they bind up and stop you from being able to do your BJJ gi pants up tight.


The way that they always seem to come loose at the worse possible time.


Finding yourself trying to hold them up with one hand or spreading your legs like you are about to get on a horse to avoid an embarrassing situation while grappling training or even worse in competition.


We hated it so much that we decided to do something about it and created “The Gi String Company” - the saviour to many problems.

 

Not our intention but at the time we also created a movement and following against the horrid flat string.
