Our Cigars

Royal Habanos currently offers a range of cigars under 21 brand names with each in turn offering a range of cigars and various packages and quantities. You will find the cigar just for you amongst our tried and tested range of high quality and authenticity guaranteed products.

The range on offer is a great cross section of Cuban cigar excellence. We have the two top contenders for the world’s best in Cohiba – a somewhat rare brand due to the high quality demanded – Castro’s favourite and Trinidad which is very similar and in equally high demand. Both are expensive but well worth it.

There are a number of brands that were originally created for specific markets – Punch, Bolivar and Montecristo for the British smoker; H. Upman, a strong, sweet cigar for the German market and Qua d Orsey (mild) and Diplomaticos (a good value hand made strong flavoured cigar) for the French.

Many brands have global success like Romeo y Julieta (an international best seller) and Cuaba a popular mild cigar with an interesting blend of flavors – great for the first time smoker as it is an easy cigar to enjoy and offers great value.

So whatever your tastes or budget are we at Royal Habanos will have the cigar for you. Easy to order, easy to pay and a guaranteed delivery is on its way.

Athenticity

A Cuban Cigar is only a true Royal Habano Cigar only when it meets all our stringent criteria.

We check the authenticity of each supplier to make sure that he is using only true Cuban tobacco leaf – from the particular area that the brand specifies – and that all the quality checks are in place to make sure that the Cuban Authority Standards are met and exceeded.

We are extremely selective of the brands that we offer and we will only send fresh cigars that have been correctly produced, packaged, stored and delivered thus insuring you of the best possible smoking experience. After all you have worked hard for this pleasure and we work as hard to give you that much sought-after authentic Cuban cigar. At Royal Habanos we are proud to be able to consistently do this.

All Cigars made and packaged in Cuba will be sealed with a hologrammed, watermarked and bar coded tape which cannot be removed and re used nor can it easily be duplicated. The bottom of each box must also carry the stamp “Habanos SA, Hecho En Cuba” and if hand crafted then also “Totalmente a Mano”. There will also be a date stamp showing when the cigars were packaged.
