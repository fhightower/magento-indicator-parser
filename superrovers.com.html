  • Is it legal to use the SuperRover on streets of Singapore?

  • Absolutely! The SuperRover has a very compact footprint, a little wider than an average person's shoulders. It can be used very easily on sidewalks, streets and other public places. Just be sure to watch out for traffic and pedestrians.

  • How does it handle wet weather?
  • SuperRover is IP54 rated which means it can handle dust and splashes of water. We strongly recommmend not riding in inclement weather or near puddles.
  • How long does it take to reach a full charge?
  • The SuperRover takes 3 hours to fully charge. Please do not charge your SuperRover for longer then 3 hours if you want to maximise its battery life.
  • How does it handle bumps, cracks and curbs - is there any kind of suspension?
  • Although there isn't a suspension system, these strong tires are no joke! The SuperRover can handle bumps, cracks and other small changes of surface up to 1-2cm very well. We recommend paying close attention to your surroundings & bending your knees, as your knees act as your natural suspension.
