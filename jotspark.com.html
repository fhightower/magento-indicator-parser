What is JotSpark?

JotSpark is a modern twist on the good ‘ole fashioned handwritten note. Made in the USA with sugar cane paper, JotSpark gives you the power to make someone’s day in the real world. It’s the tangible tool you need to “like” real things and pass the positivity. And guess what! Doing nice things for others makes the world a more positive place. Sometimes, small words of kindness can be the spark we need to brighten our day. So go ahead and spark someone’s day, spark an idea or spark a movement. Like, Dig & Love your world with JotSpark!
