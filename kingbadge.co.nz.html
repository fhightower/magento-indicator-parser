ABOUT KING BADGE

King Badge has personalised badges for every occasion. Whether you want name badges, button badges, luggage tags, pet tags for your dog or cat, service award tags, funeral or memorial buttons, or reusable name tags then King Badge will have exactly what you are after.

Located in Auckland, King Badge has been providing custom designed badges for the past 30 years. We cater for personal to large commercial orders. Our dedicated team includes an in-house graphic designer to ensure that all projects have a quick turnaround and meets expectations.

But our extensive range doesnt stop there. We also have plaques, printed T shirts, personalised clocks, coasters, rosettes, and elegant engraved plates, plus a badge machines which can be hired at very reasonable rates. King Badge is based in Auckland and supplies badges, tags and other engraved and etched products throughout NZ and elsewhere.
