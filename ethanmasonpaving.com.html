Why natural Stone

We Don't Do Reproduced. We just do REAL.

Natural stone has been used for thousands of years and will last even longer. Every single stone has its own characteristics, whether it be colour and/or texture. Its uniqueness comes from a natural process that has occurred over millions of years.
There is a vast range of beautiful natural stone to meet everyone’s individual style, taste and personality. Making it timeless.
Natural stone is strong and hardwearing. Ethan Mason’s natural stone is always calibrated for consistent thickness to help with faster installation, and when fitted, a perfect finish.

At Ethan Mason Paving we are passionate about natural stone. Your outside living space should be an extension of your home, a place you can enjoy. Whether you are looking to host family BBQs or have a private escape next to the patio heater, we are here to make that dream become reality.
