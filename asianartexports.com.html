About Us

Asian Art Exports is one of the biggest high quality manufacturers of wooden and industrial furniture as well as small items and handicrafts. Our main office (7 km from Aerodrome) in Jodhpur, Rajasthan is a cornerstone of Indian heritage handicraft production. Our experience spans into decades in wooden furniture and finishing as well as iron, home decor, textiles, housewares, and items for practical office usage. All wood is kiln treated and seasoned with a guarantee of no insects or expansion and contraction. We are fully government forest department approved.
