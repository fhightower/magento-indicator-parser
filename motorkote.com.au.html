    MOTORKOTE
      MotorKote Hyper Lubricant is a friction fighting premium synthetic. This heavy duty formulation is proven to treat metal on metal wear in any mechanical part. It creates a surface layer by bonding to metal, reducing friction and heat. This reduction in friction saves you money.
