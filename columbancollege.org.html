    Forging Partnership and Collaboration with UPHSD, Molino

    Columban College, Inc., Olongapo City entered into a Memorandum of Understanding with the University of Perpetual Help System DALTA, Molino, Cavite on June 22, 2016. The partnership covers collaboration of graduate and undergraduate students for study and research, collaboration of professors and researchers for research, lectures and discussions and sharing of information including the sharing of library materials and research publications, among others.
