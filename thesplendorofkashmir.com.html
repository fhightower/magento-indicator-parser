  • "The Splendor of Kashmir"

  • The Splendor of Kashmir is a sincere endeavor by Mrs. Varuna Anand to promote the age-old art of Shawl-making from Kashmir. They bring to you the finest and best of Kashmiri workmanship.

    It is an endeavor which balances not only the promotion of the art of shawl-making but also focuses on the well-being of the ever-so deserving craftsmen.

    In many ways they are building bridges between the creative craftsmen and the genuine lovers/patrons of the famed art of shawl-making from Kashmir.

    "Shriji Arvind Singh Mewar ,The Maharana of Udaipur"
