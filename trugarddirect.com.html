Traditionally, a manufacturer makes items and sells them to a distributor. The distributor, in turn, sells the product to retailers. Each step in the chain increases the end cost of the item, and it also serves as insurance for the step before. This is how Trugard Direct is able to keep costs down by cutting out the middle man and pass the savings onto you.
