Private Label Cosmetics Corp. is the premier source of the world's finest botanicals, including but not limited to essential oils, carrier oils, clays, toners, and botanical serums. Located in Ronkonkoma, New York, Private Label Cosmetics Corp. directly imports them from all over the world, including Chile, Italy, France, Bulgaria, India, Morocco, and China, and process them in its factory in New York. 

Private Label Cosmetics Corp.'s essential oils are 100% pure with no diluents or additives, and the webpage of each essential oil shows the extraction methods, material used, data reports, purity, and quality.   We provide high quality organic and/or 100% natural botanicals to the public at very competitive prices, with an emphasis on the pure aesthetics and freshness.  We have always put an emphasis on conducting business in an ethical, responsible, clear, and ecological way. 

Private Label Cosmetics Corp. guarantees that its products are fresh, fragrant and colorful and unlike traditional suppliers of herbal products who keep their merchandise stored in warehouses for several years.
