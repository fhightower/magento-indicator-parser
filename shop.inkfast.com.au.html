About Inkfast

Inkfast Pty Ltd ABN 11 072 441 797 is an Australian company based in Melbourne. We supply quality brand name and generic ink and toner for laser and inkjet printers.

Our brand name ink and toner is only purchased from authorised wholesalers, to ensure maximum quality. You are welcome to call for assistance or email or use the query form on the website. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

We carry an extensive range and usually ship the same day if you order by noon.
