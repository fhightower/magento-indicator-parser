SnowPaw Store offers high-quality gear for all sorts of dog-related activities, bringing together some of the best equipment from around the world.

Whether you and your dogs are a world-class dog sledding team or you enjoy spending time with your canine companion in activities such as dog scootering, backpacking or cani-cross, you'll be sure to find whatever you need to kit out your active dog.

As well as specialty dog gear for very active dogs, we also offer equipment for more the leisurely hounds, along with food and nutritional supplements, gifts and Iditarod memorabilia.

Dog scooters

If your dog is full of energy or loves to run and pull, dog scootering might be the perfect new sport for you and your pet.  You can use a dog scooter with virtually any fit and healthy dog and since the dog is harnessed in and working harder than with just a standard lead, he'll get more satisfaction out of the activity and you won't have to tug on the lead. The sport of dog scootering can also help to increase the bond between yourself and your canine companion. SnowPaw Store is the manufacturer and distributor of Pawtrekker Dog Scooters.

Complete kits are available for single-dog scootering and double-dog scootering

Sled dog equipment

Our range of sled dog equipment includes everything you'll need for racing or recreational mushing, including harnesses, ganglines and tugs. Pre-assembled ganglines are available for one, two, three, four or six dogs and all sections are made from hollow braid polyethylene, which is extremely tough, very flexible and resistant to stretching and abrasions.

Active dog gear

Help protect the active dog in your life with our range of active dog gear. For sea-faring pups, a Float Coat - a life jacket for your pooch - will help to keep your dog afloat if he unexpectedly falls overboard. We also offer dog boots to protect paws from cold or rough terrain and backpacking gear, allowing your canine to carry his own food and water on long treks. For those of you who enjoy cycling with your dog, we offer bikejoring equipment so you can go cycling with your dog.

CaniCross

For those wanting to try CaniCross (cross-country running with your dog), we have a wide range of gear. Items available include kits for single-dog and double-dog teams.

Dog walking

We also offer a wide range of high-quality and fashionable dog walking gear, including collars and leads, walking belts and dog walking harnesses. Available in a wide range of sizes and colours, you can be sure to find whatever your dog needs.

Food and health

It is important that every dog receives a balanced and healthy diet and this is a vital necessity for active dogs. Our range of dog food and nutritional supplements will help to ensure that your canine companion receives not only the best quality food, but also all of the vitamins and minerals needed for top performance.

Iditarod memorabilia, gifts and more

If you're looking for a gift for the dog lover in your life, try our range of gifts, which includes a variety of Iditarod memorabilia. Whether they're dog sledding enthusiasts, Husky fans or simply enjoy the company of their four-legged friends, you're sure to find a gift they'll love!
