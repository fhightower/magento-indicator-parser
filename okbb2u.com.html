About Us

 

OKBB Sdn. Bhd. was formerly known as Lita Bright Sdn. Bhd. incorporated in the year 2000. The company’s business operations were based on the trading of full range baby products in the local market.

The success of the factory then lead to the establishment of a corporate office, including show room, corporate office and warehouse located at Bandar Bukit Puchong in the year 2009. By then, the number of company staff had grown to over 300 talented individuals, including in-house designers, administrative staffs as well as highly trained sales and marketing personnel.

 

In October 2011, the company underwent a massive rebranding exercise and the company was renamed to OKBB Sdn. Bhd.  Following that, OKBB effectively widened its product line by becoming the official licensee of Pocoyo, Pororo, Disney Classic Pooh and Disney Classic Collection as well. In the third quarter of 2013, the company further expanded operations with the establishment of the OKBB2U.com website as a reply to the growing market trend of Internet shopping in Malaysia. During that same year, OKBB was also officially certified with ISO9001 compliance.

 

Today, the OKBB brand has become one of the major players in retail and production of baby products in the country, ranging from baby apparels to feeding accessories, gift sets, bedding and toiletries products.

 

OKBB bestselling feeding bottles, pacifiers and nipples are also certified with EN 14350 standards and conform to the European Standard which refers to Drinking Equipment for Children. Our product range also carries the mark of Children’s Product Certificate (CPC) which indicates compliance to children’s product safety rules, standards and regulations based on third party testing.

 

As a conclusion, OKBB will consistently provide young mothers with the best baby products available at all times throughout the various stages of her child’s development. OKBB have had over a decade a great success thus far, and we are looking forward to further strengthening the OKBB brand across the domestic and international market such that it will become the number one household name in baby products for many more generations to come.
