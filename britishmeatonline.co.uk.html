Dolmeinir Meats

We are a traditional mixed and non-intensive family run farm based in the heart of Anglesey. We produce, supply and deliver the finest quality meats.

Here you will find an extensive range of wholesome fresh meat. We only sell meat that has been produced by farms that follow traditional farming methods, that are kind to the natural environment.

We produce meat that is excellent in both quality and taste. We believe passionately in rearing animals naturally, to provide our customers with premium quality traceable meat.

The butchers are on hand to assist with all your requirements. If you are looking for a speciality cut, please do not hesitate to contactus.
