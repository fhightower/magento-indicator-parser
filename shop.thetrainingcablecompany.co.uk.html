Information

TCC is best known for our Training Cable brand of cables throughout the training sectors. We are now proud to announce a vast expansion of product lines covering all aspects of construction.

Our aim is to provide an excellence in customer service and reliability, leaving our customers safe in the knowledge that we deliver, in time, on time, every time.

TCC is a subsidiary of TACSA Limited. Registered in England. Company no: 8615996. Registered Address: 27 Breckhill Road, Nottingham, NG5 4GP

Prior to the time of our incorporation and many visits since, to further education establishments, we understand how and why education budgets have been cut by up to 50% in some cases.  In our response to this, we have continued to work closely with colleges, training centres and customers by using our ability to “think outside the box” to quickly generate and adapt quirky and innovative ideas to help save colleges both time and money.  

As a company we pride ourselves with these skills, and fully understand that the trades training sector, is completely different to their respective industries, as product and consumable re-cycling is paramount to the primary factor in cost cutting.

So if you have a problem and no-one else can help, you should contact the “TCC” Team.


Our unique company set up enables us to be extremely flexible, allowing us to bend and move quickly into problem solving and product sourcing, and by keeping our overheads to an absolute minimum, this allows us to pass on to our customers beneficial importer and distributor discounted prices.

Since the Training Cable brand was launched 4 years ago, our wings have spread into all areas of construction, namely electrical, plumbing, gas, decorating and building.  Our product list is vast and varied, and as raw material costs such as copper, steel and lead etc. fluctuate on a daily basis, we know that offering a price on application quotation service allows us to make available the best up to the minute prices for all our customers giving them peace of mind that if our prices fall so do theirs.

For many suppliers we have become the gateway for them to channel their products into education and many offer unique offers to us through their desire to promote themselves in what they see as an emerging market.

Here at TCC we do not have a customer service department, it is our attitude, we strive to offer an unparalleled quality and friendly service and pride ourselves on predominantly dedicating our company to supplying consumable products to colleges and training centres.

Whether you are a new or existing customer, we like to think you will be amazed at what we are able to offer.
