Lawn Mowers & Garden Machinery at Low Prices

A family run business with over thirty years experience in selling and servicing lawn mowers and gardening machinery. Our well trained staff offer impartial advice on the equipment most suited to your needs. We are one of the largest lawnmower and garden machinery retailers in South Wales located just off the A465 (Heads of the Valleys Road) in Brynmawr. We stock an extensive range of new, branded machinery, all at competitive prices.

We have fully trained mechanics to deal with all your servicing needs in our fully fitted workshops. We provide sales, spare parts and servicing on all types of domestic and professional machinery from compact tractors and lawn mowers to chainsaws, shredders to brushcutters, water pumps and generators.
