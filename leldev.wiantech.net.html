Size Chart - Finding Your Perfect Fit

Our clothes are not made bigger, just taller. All of our inseams are 36" unless otherwise noted. Please consult our misses size chart below to see what size will fit best, based on your measurements. In general we recommend ordering your regular size. If a particular item is running small, large, long or short, we will let you know about this at the end of the item description.

Extended sizes (XXL, 22 & 24 available in many styles)
BUST — With arms relaxed down at sides, keep tape parallel to the floor and measure around the fullest part of your bust.
WAIST — Bend to one side to find the natural crease of your waist. Measure at this point, making sure the tape fits comfortably around your waist.
HIPS — Stand with heels together and measure around the fullest part of your hips.
INSEAM — Measure a similar style pant that fits you well; Measure from the crotch seam to the hem.
