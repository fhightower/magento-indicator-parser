Resetting Webcam Permissions

The online measuring tool requires the use of your webcam to determine your PD. If your webcam isn't working or you accidentally denied access when prompted by your web browser,follow the instructions below to change the allowance.

VIEW INSTRUCTIONS FOR:

 
  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Internet Explorer

Chrome

When webcam access is requested a grey bar will appear below the address bar, you need to click Allow in order to continue. if you hit Deny, then you need to reset the allowance.

A small camera icon will appear in the right side of the address bar of your browser. Click on the camera and select Ask if MeasureMyPD.com wants to access your camera in the drop-down window followed by Done. Press the Reload button when prompted or simply Refresh your Page, and click Allow when prompted again.

Firefox

Firefox requests webcam access through a drop-down dialog in the top-left area of your browser window. You need to select your webcam from the drop-down and click Share Selected Device.If this window was hidden or you selected not to share ,simply find and click on the camera icon to the left of the website address and change your selection.

Internet Explorer

You will see one of two dialog windows appear inside the Flash application.Depending on which you see you will either choose the Allow option and click Done, or just click the Allow button.

If you hit Deny,simply right-click inside the application window and choose Settings to make the window reappear.
