Be.Lead.Do!TM is a mobile-ready online leadership resource, organized into concise 20-minute interactive modules — small blocks of time you can make a habit of fitting in every day to help you avoid costly missteps, improve your performance as a leader, and ultimately advance your career.

Making smart decisions under pressure. Hiring and growing the right talent. Inspiring excellence. Executing successful strategies. Skills like these are at the core of effective leadership. And with Be.Lead.Do!, mastering them is only a few clicks away.

The experience of training 375,000 employees in 21 countries and running hundreds of leadership programs worldwide for 12 years inspired Be.Lead.Do! creator Lior Arussy to design a unique vehicle for imparting the essentials of leadership to busy managers. Working with clients such as Mercedes-Benz, Microsoft, Citi, Johnson & Johnson, FedEx and many others as President and CEO of Strativity Group — a global research and consulting firm — Arussy has gained deep insights into the workings of today's demanding business environment. These are woven into Be.Lead.Do!, along with the concise teachings of over 250 books on leadership.
