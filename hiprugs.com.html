  • Flat Weave Rugs
    Flat Weave Rugs
    • Flatweave area rugs are unique due to their expertly crafted construction method. These rugs, unlike many wool rugs which are knotted, are instead made by weaving yarn on a loom. Without the thickness from knots, these woven rugs will be flatter, coining the term flat-weave. Also, because the rugs are woven, there is no actual pile (cut yarn atop the rug) which makes for easy cleaning and vacuuming. This distinct construction offers versatility, incredible durability and with their lower cost, you can afford to change them up depending on your style of the moment.

Area rugs are one of the most popular ways to inexpensively decorate all rooms in your home. Available in all shapes, sizes, materials and colors, rugs can easily be changed and rotated according to your mood or the time of year. Wool is the most common material for indoor rugs because of its durability and versatility. Use wool area rugs in your living room, dining room, family room, hallways and entry ways. Flat weave rugs are also quickly growing in popularity because of their ease of transition. Use these patterned rugs in hallways and stair cases, outdoor kitchens, children’s rooms and other casual high traffic areas. Outdoor rugs made of bamboo, polypropylene or braided sisal and jute can make your patio, pool area, porch or hardwood deck look modern and artistic.
