Trucks Direct UK Limited, based very close to Birmingham City Centre, is one of the largest independent UK providers of used forklifts for the retail market. We have literally hundreds of used forklift trucks for sale ready in our showroom, including gas forklift trucks, electric forklift trucks and diesel forklift trucks. In addition to offering the lowest prices in the UK for used forklifts (as well as a price match guarantee not to be beaten), we also offer assistance with finance options, a 12 month LOLER certificate and a 30 day warranty, as well as a 30 day money back guarantee on all trucks which cannot be matched throughout the industry.

Our Clients

We emphasise that our fork lift trucks are all our own. We do not advertise second hand forklift trucks that we do not have or any other dealers’ stock. We welcome all customers new and old to our showroom where 100s of fork lift trucks can be viewed and are ready ship. Should one of the forklifts be unavailable in the live stock list, please enquire as we may be able to source your exact requirements. We can deliver second hand forklifts anywhere in the UK and worldwide.

At Trucks Direct UK, we offer a comprehensive range of Pyroban protected Diesel and Electric Forklifts, encompassing both Zone 1 and 2 Protection. A quality used, low-houred Pyroban machine can save businesses thousands of pounds against the expense of new equipment, but still providing the same levels of protection.
