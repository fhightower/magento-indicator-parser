More About Shed Dog Training!

Shed Dog training has become a widely popular hobby that has extended the lifestyle activity of the passionate bird dog owner or whitetail hunter in the off-season. More recently, shed antler hunting by using a dog, trained specifically for this purpose, has taken the sport to a whole new level. Popularized by our good friends Jeremy Moore (Dog Bone Hunter Shed Products) and Tom Dokken (nationally acclaimed hunting gun dog trainer and founder of (Dokken) Dead Fow Trainer, Shed Dog Trainer products, Oak Ridge Kennels, and N.A.S.H.D.A (The North American Shed Hunting Dog Association) shed hunting with our loyal hunting dog combines the best of both worlds. Our passion runs deep like yours, the love of finding big game antler sheds and the companionship and rewards of working our gun dogs in the field.

We are focusing our shed dog training products that are about quality, not quantity. Any product or brand that we carry on Everything Shed Dog was been personally used by us or our pro staff and we have fully endorsed this products as top quality and reliable. We do not want to be everything to everyone, but the highest quality outlet for products and information associated with shed dog hunting!  And Yes, I said information, we don't just want to provide product, we want this site to be your #1 destination for information and events and activities in the shed dog community.  Please contact us if you have an event you would like us to post, we are here to spread the word and grow this great sport!

We carry the most extensive line-up of shed dog training products and shed antler dog hunting products by top manufactures such Dokken Shed dog trainer Products, DogBone Hunter Products, Remington Dog Products, SportDOG, Tri-Tronics, and many more!! Be sure to checkout our general dog products as well, as we want you to be fully equipped when you are afield with your best friend while antler dog training and hunting.
