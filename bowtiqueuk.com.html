About Bowtique

Bowtique is known for providing its on-point fashion bags and accessories at unbelievable prices. We pride ourselves on quality, prices and most importantly - style.  From humble beginnings, Bowtique has expanded with stores scattered across the North West and Merseyside and continues to thrive and grow.

We aim to give our customers a fantastic shopping experience both in store and online. We believe in the art of shopping and want to ensure our customers leave our stores feeling great and excited about their new purchase. 

We are constantly updating our range and adding new designs so our customers get the latest fashions and the best prices.
