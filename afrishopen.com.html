FAQs
What is Afrishopen
Afrishopen is Africa largest Online Shopping Mall where buyers and sellers meet to make awesome deals. At Afrishopen, we pride ourselves in offering the best prices online in Africa for nearly all items sold ranging from fashion and electronics to jewelry and books. Afrishopen is the one stop shop for buyers looking for top deals and sellers looking to rock their sales.
Why should I choose Afrishopen?
Afrishopen Online Shopping Community comprises of millions of genuine buyers and sellers completing thousands of transactions every day. Sellers on Afrishopen are based across the country bringing in wide variety of products ranging from fashion and electronics to Jewelries and books offering the best price in the market. As a buyer, whether you are looking for an inexpensive used phone or a brand new laptop, a dress for your wife or an awesome tie for your boyfriend, you can find a selection that works for you with convenient payment methods and reliable product delivery.
How does it work?
Buying or selling on Afrishopen is a simple process. All that you need to do is to register for an account to get started. Simply follow the steps below to complete your registration today! Click Login/Register button on the top of the homepage. Click Create a new account under the new member section. Fill up the form to complete the registration.
How do i buy on Afrishopen?
Please review the section above to find a product of your choice. -Then click on the product name to view more details (price, size, color and condition etc.).

- Once you are sure you want to buy, click Buy from seller (in your desktop) or Buy (in your mobile device) to proceed to the checkout. Review the price and the shipping cost before completing the purchase.

Finally, click Checkout (in your desktop) or Confirm your Purchase (in your mobile device) to place an order with the seller.

You will receive an email with a link. Click on Order Confirmation in the email in order to confirm your purchase.

Once you have validated your purchase, the seller will contact you to arrange delivery.
