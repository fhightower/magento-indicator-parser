Welcome Message

Welcome to De Novo Chocolates.  When you enjoy De Novo Chocolates, you make an ordinary day extraordinary.

Our chocolates are vegan, gluten free, all natural, and made wtih fair trade certified cacao beans.  Unlike mass production of chocolate candies, De Novo Chocolates are all about flavor, nutrition and freshness.  Each piece is hand crafted with the appreciation of art and healthy life style.  It is made with the health conscious consumers in mind.  We constantly create new combinations of ingredients and offer new products as more functional food are being identified by researchers.  This is why our company is named De Novo.  It means our chocolates are anew and afresh.

After enjoying our chocolates you will recognize that De Novo Chocolates has raised the standard in quality, freshness and nutritious value in chocolates.  We make no compromise when it comes to what we put into our chocolates.  We strive to meet the standard of the health conscious and fitness enthusiasts.  We welcome your comments so we can keep improving and creating new nutritious foods for you to share and enjoy.
