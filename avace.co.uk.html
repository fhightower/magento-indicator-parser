about us

We listen carefully to customers, to understand their business processes and identify their current and future needs. Our goal is to exceed customer expectations in relation to performance, value, communication and service. Our customer care team are knowledgeable, decisive and have the autonomy to communicate both timely and effectively with our customers.

Our products meet internationally recognised quality standards, manufactured in efficient facilities with a commitment to continuous improvement, utilising lean methodology to streamline processes, increase productivity and reduce waste. 

Stock powders are available in any quantity for express next day delivery or timed delivery throughout the UK. Custom and non standard powders  are available through our fast track small batch made to order service, available in any colour, gloss or special effect.

A comprehensive range of Protective Coatings certified to meet the requirements of Norsok, Network Rail and Highways Agency complete the product range. Manufactured in house in a broad range of colours, available for express delivery throughout the UK.

For those customers that seek value we will deliver a unique customer experience, combining a consultative relationship with experience and knowledge. Delivering competitively positioned high quality performance products for our customers.
