Cuttle Fish and Corals

Saltwater Fish, Corals, Tanks and Service

Cuttle Fish and Corals is able to ensure its clients that the fish and corals are healthy through its insistence on fair collection and aquaculturing practices.

All tanks come with most advanced technology available to create the perfect aquarium, including LED lighting and the cutting edge filtration systems. 

We only sell sustainably collected and aquacultured fish, corals and invertebrates. We work with suppliers and fishermen who reflect the sustainability mandate as well as distributors and harvesters who use responsible and sustainable collecting methods.
