Why choose Dodson Group?

At Dodson Group, our customers' bottom line is what's important. As a family run business, we understand what a challenge it can be for businesses to find supply partners they can count on to provide consistent customer service and affordable pricing.

That's why we strive not only to provide the most competitive pricing on marketing materials, automotive and business supplies available, but also unparalleled customer support. When you call to place an order, you will speak with our knowledgeable sales reps directly - no navigating automated phone systems.

We offer a wide umbrella of quality business services, marketing materials and solutions, from automotive forms and direct mail fulfillment to business card printing and payment solutions. By choosing us as a supplier, businesses can consolidate purchasing to one single supplier, cutting costs and wasted time dealing with multiple vendors.

Many services. One source. Consistently great service and pricing. The auto dealer supply specialists.

Having specialized in car dealership supplies for almost two decades, our expertise and pricing are some of the best in the industry. Auto dealerships will find everything they need in our comprehensive catalog of automotive forms and accessories, paddle flags, GPS auto tracking devices and more. When it comes to direct mailing solutions, we specialize in custom postcards and print mailings for auto dealership events.
