Welcome to Gippsland Safety Supplies

The SWF Group has been formed from a number of Australia's leading Workwear & Safety Companies combining forces to enable more Resource to be focused on Marketing, Buying and Customer Service.

These companies have been originated since 1987 and with the owners being the man on the floor, bring together a wealth of experience. The combined experience that comes with working together to satisfy our customers is a great advantage in working towards our Mission.

The Website you are viewing now is a product of the more focused vision we have to ensure that our customers have access to everyday Safety & Workwear needs without delay.

When you choose One of The SWF Group as your source of workplace supplies, you can be assured of great range, great value and great service provided by our team.
