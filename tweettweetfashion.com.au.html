Australian Shipping

For all orders over $60* we offer free express shipping to Australian customers through Startrack.

Australian orders are shipped through Star track Express via overnight service as standard, for all metro areas order should be received within 1-2 business days.

All orders shipped to a PO Box address will be sent via Australia Post Express and must be ordered by 1pm (AEST) for next day delivery (subject to aus post next day delivery network).

If you're located outside of metro areas please allow up to 5 business days to receive your order.

If you do not receive your order after 5 business days please email us at info@tweettweetfashion.com.au with your order details and we will look into it straight away!

 

New Zealand Shipping

All shipping prices are listed in Australian Dollars (AUD) including GST.

We offer a $15.00 flat rate shipment to New Zealand via Express Courier International.

For all orders over $120 we offer free shipping, enter the code NZSHIP at the checkout  to redeem free shipping.

All New Zealand orders are sent with Australia Post Express Courier International, estimated delivery time is 2-5 business days, but allow up to 2 weeks during busy periods.

All estimated shipping times exclude if your order is held up whilst in customs, this is beyond our control.

 

International Shipping

All shipping prices are listed in Australian Dollars (AUD) including GST.

We offer a flat delivery rate of $25 for standard international shipping, or $40 for express international shipping.

Standard international orders are sent with Australia Post Pack & Track, estimated delivery time is 3-4 weeks, but allow up to 5 weeks during busy periods.

Express international orders are sent with Australia Post Express Courier International, estimated delivery time is 4-10 days but allow up to 2 weeks during busy periods.

All estimated shipping times exclude if your order is held up whilst in customs, this is beyond our control. 

For more specific shipping times to your destination please contact us at info@tweettweetfashion.com.au

 

Pre-Order Shipping

Items are expected to arrive within 1 to 2 weeks after purchase, we will endevour to get the items as soon as possible.

Once we post your item you will recieve a shipping confirmation email with your tracking details. 

For more information on Pre-orders, please read our terms and conditions here

 

Customs duties and taxes for New Zealand and International Orders

We will do our best to avoid and reduce the customs duties and taxes payable on all New Zealand and International orders. However, in the event that an international shipment attracts custom duties and taxes, the customer is required to pay all the Customs, Duties and Taxes on the order. 
 

Tracking your order

Once your order has been placed you will receive a 'Processing' email notification letting you that your order has been packed and will be sent out that day.

If you wish to be notified of your tracking number (consignment note) then please email us at info@tweettweetfashion.com.au to request this.

If you are not home at the time of delivery, you will be left a sorry we missed you card and your parcel will be redirected to your local post office. Please take the card and your photo ID to your local post office for collection.



All Purchases made on weekends and public holidays are processed on the next business day.

Please note shipping times do not include processing and handling time, please allow 2-3 days prior to shipment.

Once your parcel has been collected by our couriers and we have proof of shipping, we are no longer held responsible for the parcel.

*Free shipping will not work in conjunction with store credits, to be entitled to the discount your checkout total must be $60 or more.
