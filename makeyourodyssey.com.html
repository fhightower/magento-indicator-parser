CUT FOR COMFORT

Make Your Odyssey’s Lido Cut swimming shorts have been developed with style and comfort in mind. Retaining a fitted silhouette whilst employing a relaxed elasticated waistband finish for extra comfort.

Learn more

QUICK DRY JACQUARD WOVEN FABRIC

Our Lido Cut shorts are made using bright coloured yarns and woven into graphical patterns such as the Parasols and Shells, evoking thoughts of summer living and strolls along the shore. Jacquard woven, never printed in order to achieve richer more vibrant colours that remain. All of our swimwear is woven using Italian nylon in custom patterns, made exclusively for Make your Odyssey. The result is a fabric that is beautiful on the eye, cool, light, breathable and quick to dry.

INTERNALLY DRAINING POCKETS

Keen not to interrupt the silhouette and compromise on appearance, Make Your Odyssey have integrated pockets to drain water and air from the shorts internally and effectively.

DETAILING

With attention to detail in mind the Lido shorts feature custom engraved M.Y.O hexagonal pocket buttons and are further embellished with Parasol engraved cord ends, the Parasol representing life Above the Surf. All custom metallic accessories used by Make Your Odyssey are made from zamak, a non conductive metal which will not heat up in the sun preventing any accidental burns.

QUALITY INSIDE AND OUT

Make Your Odyssey swimwear is lined using a treated nylon netting. Made in Italy, the netting is lightweight, non-sweat and anti-bacterial. An important component of the shorts, the netting is made to ensure you remain fresh and comfortable throughout the day, be it having a swim before enjoying lunch at the beach or long days out exploring a new coastline.
