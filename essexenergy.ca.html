Welcome to
Essex Energy

Essex Energy is a dynamic energy technology company.  As the Smart Grid is evolving in North America and throughout the world, so are the needs of electrical utilities, generators, transmitters, and consumers.  For the past 13 years, we’ve taken pride in providing solutions for these diverse needs.  Our products and services include Distribution Engineering, Renewable Energy Development, Distributed Generation Aggregation, Conservation & Demand Management, and Smart Grid Software Products & Development.  First class services provided by first class employees.  At Essex Energy, our employees are our greatest asset.  Let us help you today.
