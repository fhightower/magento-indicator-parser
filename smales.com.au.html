Fine jewellery and luxury timepieces since 1937



Smales is one of Western Australia's most experienced jewellers and watchmakers. Our expertise in diamond jewellery, engagement rings and luxury Swiss watches is unrivalled in the state. We are an Official Retailer of Rolex, Breitling, Tudor and Seiko watches. Hearts On Fire diamond jewellery - 'The World's Most Perfectly Cut Diamond' - is exclusive to Smales in WA. We also design and handcraft bespoke jewellery and offer a complete jewellery and watch service. Browse a huge selection of our jewellery and watches online or visit us today at one of our stunning showrooms in Perth, Bunbury or Kalgoorlie.
