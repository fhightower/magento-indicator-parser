About us

Neo Interiors Modern Furniture Store
Our concept at NEO Furniture is to offer an exciting array of modern and contemporary home furnishings for every room of your home. While our showroom may not be that large, our selection of furniture is endless. We represent over 70 manufacturers from Italy, Spain, Belgium and other European countries. Most companies have at least one item on our sales floor, and our website has over 12,000 items for you to choose from. Over time, we've expanded our merchandise categories to also include lighting, rugs and accessories to complete your modern décor. NEO Furniture takes pride in providing a focused selection of modern furnishings that understand the way you really live. Furniture that's easy to love, easy to maintain and, best of all, easy on your budget. Modern Furniture that lives the way you do.
