Welcome To Boi Trading


A leading supplier of mens and boys casual wear. We have extensive styles of Jeans, Trousers, Jackets, T-shirts, Sweatshirts, Knitwear and Fashion Tops. All of which have been designed and manufactured to the highest quality using the latest fabrics and techniques.

We are situated approximately 5 minutes drive from Manchester City Centre on Bury New Rd. Boi Trading has been established for over 20 years, with a wide range of businesses within the retail sector buying our products - from small independents to large store groups.

Our smaller customers enjoy choosing products from our stock ranges, whilst for our larger customers we offer a more bespoke design and manufacturing service. Larger customers who utilize the bespoke service have their needs taken care of by our team of designers and merchandisers. They will help you design a range and organise sampling, production and delivery on your behalf, taking the hassle out of creating a brand for your stores. They can even help you to merchandise your range in house with point of sale advertising and visual displays to create that impressive branded image.

Fashion is constantly changing and evolving with innovative new designs and exciting fabrics emerging every season. Boi Trading Company keeps abreast of the changes as they happen mid-season we build them into our program by sourcing factories which can give us a speedy high quality production with the shortest possible lead times. This way our garments hit the high street as it’s happening and not when its over.
