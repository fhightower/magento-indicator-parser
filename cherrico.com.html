Welcome to Cherrico Furniture. We offer solid hardwood furniture hand crafted in America to the high standards of the American home.

Our furniture is constructed from six premium hardwood species: oak, quarter sawn white oak, cherry, maple, walnut and elm.

Our Products are available in many environmentally friendly stain colors and offer you the opportunity to customize them to
fit your decorating and practical needs. Cherrico Furniture provides the quality, style, personality and adaptability you should
expect in furnishing your fine home.
