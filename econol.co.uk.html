Clean, flueless and eco friendly – we’ve got it covered.

Our Premium grade bioethanol is designed for fireplaces offering a beautiful flame which dance and flicker, drawing you in so you can sit and lose yourself in the shapes and colours.

If you have a room which is crying out for the focal point of a beautiful fireplace, you can now install a bioethanol fire which is fueled by our bioethanol which requires no vents or chimneys. With a totally clean burn with no harmful emissions, bioethanol is the fuel of the future.

Bioethanol is a sustainable and renewable energy source made by fermenting by-products of the agricultural industry which would otherwise go to waste. It burns cleanly, without any fumes, giving off nothing more than heat, steam and a small amount of Carbon Dioxide.
