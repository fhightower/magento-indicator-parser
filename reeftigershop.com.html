A Review Of Zenith Pilot Type 20 Hommageá Louis Blériot Mens Watch

If you love transparency in your timepieces, this beauty is bound to meet your taste. Its 47 mm case is made of sapphire, and only has a few key components made of 18kt white gold: the crown, the bezel and the lugs. The sapphire features anti-reflexive coating to permit excellent visibility of both the dial and the movement. Its remarkable transparency makes the watch seem almost weightless, which is very fitting, given that it was created as a tribute to French aviator Louis Blériot.
