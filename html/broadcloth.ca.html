Broadcloth.ca - Canada's Online Broadcloth Store
Broadcloth.ca is a Toronto, ON, Canada based subsidiary  and online arm of a local Canadian fabric wholesaler. The prices you see for the full bolts of broadcloth are true wholesale prices, available for direct purchase on this site. For those needing smaller quantities, broadcloth by the yard is available as well. Broadcloth.ca carries a wide selection of broadcloth colours to cover all you broadcloth needs.

No registration required to get a shipping quote from the shopping cart.
