Jazzmin is not just a label, but also a reflection of our craftsmen’s creativity and fervor for making and designing cashmere pashmina garments that brings out the best of a person. As a fashion brand and manufacturer of pashmina apparels, Jazzmin specializes in all type of Pashmina clothes like scarf, shawls, kurties, caftan, jackets and many more work for men and women. Jazzmin also specializes in handmade works such as Zari, Dabka, Zardozi, Aari, Nakatikki, etc. Jazzmin sells their products by online shopping at www.onlinecashmerescarf.com at very reasonable rates worldwide with free shipping facility.

Jazzmin’s flight did not stop here, as creativity and passion knows no border. Jazzmin took her label outside India as well. Jazzmin provides luxury cashmere pashmina fabrics to many European fashion brands. We are white label manufacture and wholesale supplier to many fashion brands. Jazzmin’s passion for fashion and her undivided focus is behind the success of Jazzmin.

Jazzmin channelized all its resources to expand their retail business in the last few years, and as a result, Jazzmin is on a new high. The label Jazzmin brings out the magic of craftsmen’s skill and designing through its collection of 100% authentic, pure and luxury scarf, shawls, kurties, jackets, caftan, stoles and much more. Jazzmin is proud to present a range of men’s and women’s apparel, which blends the best of Indian, Kashmiri and contemporary designs fused with Jazzmin’s magical craftsmanship.

Jazzmin manages to strike a perfect balance between Luxury and Simplicity , and Jazzmin makes sure that customers of their brand gets exactly what they are looking for. The label put forward tailored apparel and evening wear matching specific requirements of customers. All the apparels whether casual or formal wear; demonstrate our country’s rich heritage through best in the class textiles together with the opulence of Indian Craftsmanship in Cashmere Pashmina apparels. 

If style is what you need, style is what you get at Jazzmin.
