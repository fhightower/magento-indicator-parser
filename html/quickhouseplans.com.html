Welcome to Quickhouseplans.com!

Here you will find a wide variety fresh, new and innovate house plan designs. From a traditional two-story to a Tuscan rambler, we have plans to fit your style, taste and budget.

Our plans boast many features found in expensive, time-consuming custom designs, like practical and convenient layouts, open floor plans, bonus space and much more. Available options like adding a third car garage, fireplace, decks, walk-out basements, etc can make your home feel even more like a custom built home.

And one really cool thing about our plans; every floor plan can be built using one of 9 exterior styles:

  • Craftsman
  • French Country
  • Mediterranean
  • Modern
  • Mountain Rustic
  • Prairie
  • Southwestern
  • Traditional
  • Victorian

So don’t feel trapped by a certain exterior style or floor plan, there are multiple possibilities!

All quickhouseplans.com plans are value engineered, meaning to cut down on waste in material costs and to save you money in building expenses. Not only are we quick, but we are green too!

How does it work?

Our process is simple.

  1. 1. Search for the right houseplan to fit your needs.
  2. 2. Choose your desired changes at checkout. Each plan purchase includes 5 hours of changes.
  3. 3. Order your plan.

If you do not require any changes to the plan you ordered, a PDF version of the plan will be emailed to the following business day or we will ship out the physical plans the following business day. Just specify which you prefer upon checkout.

If you did request any changes, your plans will be emailed (PDF) or shipped (physical plans) to you the following business day after the changes are completed.

Please allow 2-3 days for most changes.
