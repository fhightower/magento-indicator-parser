Quick Pack Supplies - Your Packaging Experts

 

We have more than 5 years experience in the packaging trade. Over this time we have developed great relations with manufacturers to bring customers premium packaging whilst maintaining affordable pricing.

What do we sell?

We have developed close relations with various manufacturers enabling us to supply thousands of different products. Our most popular lines include mailing bags, cheap envelopes, small resealable grip seal bags, garment bags, board back envelopes, candy sweet party bags, bubble wrap bags and no mixed coin / tamper evident bank bags. 

Why buy from us?

  • We only provide quality packaging combined with excellent customer service.
  • We're not just a supplier, we offer advice and assistance whenever you need it. 
  • Our expert team is devoted to providing all of your packaging needs, with a prime focus on Service, Innovation and Reliability. 

Local Packaging

Apart from providing postal packaging Internationally we are passionate about our local customers too. The vast majority of our stock is stored in house and available for collection from our premises.

We're located in the heart of the West Midlands, this location allows us to provide packaging to various towns and cities including: Birmingham, Dudley, Oldbury, Tipton, West Bromwich, Walsall, Wednesbury, Smethwick,  Halesowen and Wolverhampton.
