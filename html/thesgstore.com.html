Degreaser cum Booster

The Degreaser cum Booster from Natural Care is a ph7 powerful concentrated liquid surfactant. It is highly effective in removing mineral, synthetic oil, tar, grease etc. on heavily soiled surfaces. Used in conjunction with our mainwash laundry detergent, it’s fast acting and high emulsifying power shows excellent performance on fatty/oil soil on fabrics as well. Fat & oil once removed from surfaces or fabrics, are effectively dispersed by Natural Care’s Degreaser cum Booster, thus preventing re-deposition.
