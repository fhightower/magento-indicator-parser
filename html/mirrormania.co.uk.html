Mirror Manufacturer & Artist

Welcome to our range of Beautiful and Unique Hand-crafted Mirrors and Decorative Glass from our very own British Designer Phillip Orr. Established for over 20 years, we are a British company, designing and handcrafting mirrors and decorative glass products in our Norfolk-based workshop and showroom.
