About Us

Sandbag was established in 2002 and has grown organically and independently to employ more than 50 personnel. With a second office now open in Los Angeles, CA, we offer global solutions with a personal touch to our clients.

Our team is comprised of industry experts with decades of experience from varying sectors including Grammy Award winning product designers and D2C experts and we are proud to be working with some of the world’s most respected and influential artists ranging from Adele to The xx on innovative product campaigns.
