A Little About Corks & Cases

We are independent wine merchants based in our wine shop in the heart of the Yorkshire Dales, at Masham, North Yorkshire.

Corks & Cases supply exceptional wines to our local discerning customers, restaurants, golf clubs, wine bars and pubs across the region and now our wines are online for you to buy.

At Corks & Cases you may order from our large selection of wines, securely, online, for delivery across the United Kingdom.

We aim to offer our web based customers an improved experience when buying on our new website and want to deliver you the best quality wines at affordable prices.
