Welcome to Secret Florist, an independent online florist tucked away in Mytchett, Surrey (GU16).

We arrange and deliver flowers for any occasion, with free delivery for all orders within a 5-mile radius of us.

As an online business, we appreciate that the personal touch is important; we are just a friendly ‘phone call or an email away and we welcome discussing your requirements – your satisfaction is our highest priority.

In addition to our standard arrangements of flowers, we can create any flower arrangements to your specifications: please contact us to discuss any flower arrangements that do not feature on our website.

We are also delighted to be able to supply local customers with bespoke cakes with your order, if required.*
