Welcome to Bearing Tool Centre

Bearing Tool Centre is leading wholesaler, exporter and suppliers of measuring instruments, measurements tools and equipments as well as testing tools. Established in 1981, we have earned a great credit with our services and reached a great peak even with cut-throat competition. We provide quality solutions as per client requirements. We supply a great range of measuring instruments and testing tools equipments to multiple industries. We believe in quality and functionality by providing top brands measuring tools and equipments.
