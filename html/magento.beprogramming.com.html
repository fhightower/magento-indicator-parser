About Us

Out of a passion for yarn and a craft that my Mother has passed down to me, Wool Addiction was born. Established in 2005 and located at Bowral in the beautiful Southern Highlands of NSW, Wool Addiction will inspire you with our range of exotic yarns from all over the world, inspiring pattern designs and tantalising knitting accessories, this is just the beginning of the experience that you will encounter upon entering our doors.

Wool Addiction's outstanding customer service is second to none, and our highly trained and friendly staff are always ready to assit and guide you when required. With our warm, welcoming atmosphere, we here at Wool Addiction look forward to you visitng our store soon.
