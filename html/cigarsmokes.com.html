  • Our Story

From the beginning, CigarSmokes — the Spain-based online retailer of cigars — has been devoted to making available the finest Cuban cigars.

Whether you are an aficionado of tobacco or just dabbling into the world of first-grade cigars, we have what you are looking for. Our selection of premium Cuban cigars will astound you for their richness and purity.

With 24 different brands to pick from, our customers find it difficult to choose just one variety. But they are never disappointed. We are proud to say that every cigar we offer is authentic and handcrafted, and it will always be this way.

We will always be the official Habanos duty-free retailer, providing you with the utmost quality of service. Our customers have grown accustomed to the convenience and value we offer by purchasing cigars online directly from a European retailer.
