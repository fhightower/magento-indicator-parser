AP, CLEP, and DSST

CLEP and AP exams are organized by the College Board, which is the same organization that produces the SAT.

CLEP (College Level Examination Program) is a popular, standardized test that allows students to earn college credit based on prior learning or an independent course, such as a JumpCourse. Explore our CLEP prep options.

DSST (formerly known as DANTES) is another standardized test that helps students earn college credits outside of the traditional classroom. We offer several DSST prep courses.
