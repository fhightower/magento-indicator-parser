Abbey Artstone are not a merchant or middle man, we are a direct manufacturer of high quality architectural masonry products selling direct to individuals, small businesses and large house builders. 

Based in Keighley, West Yorkshire Abbey Artstone Limited provides high quality architectural masonry products throughout the UK to customers ranging from small builders to large national house builders and contractors building multi site developments.

Abbey Artstone Limited provides four main product types. Semi dry cast stone products,Wetcast reinforced stone products. Glass Reinforced Cement (GRC) and Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP). From these four base materials we can provide a wide range of masonry products to suit your needs.

Heads, Cills, Window Surrounds, Bullseye windows, Balustrade, Copings, Lightweight copings, Tudor boarding, Door Surrounds, Porticos, these are all items that Abbey Artstone Limited produces daily for clients.

We have a full in house scheduling and detailing service to help turn your planning drawing into working production details and costings.

Please browse our site and technical documentation and feel free to contact one of our sales staff should you require any further information or assistance. We do sell a wide range of stock items on our website along with surplus stock requirements and we also offer a range of made to order items (please contact us for lead times on these items). For bespoke and special items or to discuss a project you may have, please do not hesitate to telephone, fax or email us.
