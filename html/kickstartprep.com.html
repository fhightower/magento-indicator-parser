About Kickstart Prep.

We are Simon Walsh and Hannah Griffiths, Co-Founders of SWHG Enterprises, LLC and  Kickstart Prep.   We are teen entrepreneurs who have always dreamed of blending our desire to help others with our passion for entrepreneurial ventures.  We live in Manhattan Beach, California and we are well aware of the imminent threat that earthquakes pose in our state and region.  Our desire to form Kickstart Prep stemmed from a keen interest to help people prepare for these and other types of disasters.  Our business philosophy is founded on giving back to our local community, with a percentage of our Kickstart Prep profits donated to worthy community organizations.
