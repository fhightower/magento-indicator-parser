Welcome to Best Mowers (www.bestmowers.co.uk), an online retail facility of Makerfield Limited.

Best Mowers sells a wide range of quality products, not just mowers, for gardens of all sizes, at competitive prices for delivery direct to your home. These are all sourced from established suppliers and include the Grizzly range of high quality, German engineered, garden power tools which come with a 3 year guarantee.

Lawn care products
Our lawn care products include hand mowers, electric mowers and petrol mowers so that you can select the right model to suit the size of your garden - and your pocket. For that extra special care we also offer scarifiers/lawn aerators, battery powered shears and lawn trimmers to keep the edges looking neat. And if you’re tackling a garden that’s overgrown with shrubs, saplings or grass clumps, then check out our selection of ergonomic petrol brush cutters with easy start, 2-stroke engines.

Trees and hedges
The robust, lightweight hedge trimmers on our site have high quality, laser cut, steel blades that are suitable even for thick, woody hedges. Long reach models are also available so you no longer have to balance on a rickety ladder for tall hedges up to 3.5m high. Whether powered by a re-chargeable lithium-ion battery, mains electricity or petrol, they are safe, easy to use and will take the hard work out of keeping your hedge in trim. If you have to cut through trees or shrubs, we also have a range of standard, in-line and long reach chain saws powered by efficient electric or petrol motors with high quality metal gears and blade.

When it’s time to tidy up
Also part of the Best Mowers range are combined blowers and vacuums for clearing leaves, as well as robust garden shredders with high torque, turbo powered motors to work their way quickly and efficiently through shrub cuttings, green material and branches up to 40mm in diameter. For cleaning up the patio and other jobs around the garden, a pressure washer or brush drive and patio cleaner could be just what you need. We even have a selection of pumps for pumping out clear or dirty water with suspended particles up to 35mm diameter from ponds, pools and flooded areas.

Wheelbarrows
A stylish, heavy duty plastic wheelbarrow will make light work of general purpose carrying jobs around the home and garden, so make sure there’s one from our range in your garden shed.
