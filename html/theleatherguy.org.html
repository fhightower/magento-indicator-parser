 Welcome to The Leather Guy – Leather Hides & Leather Supplies

         

 

          

 

          

 

The Leather Guy offers a massive selection of unique leather hides and leather supplies. You can browse by category, weight, color, or price to find the exact leather hide or leather supply you need.

All of our leather hides are one-of-a-kind so we provide multiple pictures of every leather hide on our website. The leather hide you see in the product photos is the exact leather hide that we'll ship directly to your doorstep. We're dedicated to providing exceptional customer service, competitive prices and timely delivery of all our leather hides and leather supplies. We assure quality and satisfaction for any leather purchase, big or small.

Need a unique leather item not listed on our website? Please call us at 507-932-3795 or drop us an email to discuss your leather needs!
