Welcome to the 2015 - 2016 Season!

 

 

Our fruit is different. It is tree-ripened. We know it's fully ripened when the fruit's skin turns yellow and has a red blush to it. Since we are the growers as well as the shippers, we leave the fruit on the tree until we are ready to fill the order. We normally pick, pack, and ship your fruit on the same day!

Many other fruit shippers do not grow the fruit they sell. The citrus is picked when green, chemically treated to change the color for the skin and ware-housed for more than a week before shipped.

IMPORTANT NOTICE: Due to USDA ruling on the "Sweet Orange Scab" we are unable to ship to Arizona.
