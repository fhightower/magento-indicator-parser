Welcome to Biharilal Holaram Jewellers

BH Store is a subsidiary of Biharilal Holaram Jewellers (Jaipur, India), and built to provide our wonderful creation to our online audience. Founded in 1941, we have gained remarkable recognition as BH, and have earned reputation in the Gemstone industry.

Sri Holaram Lakhi started the gemstone family business, and the family has followed his footsteps for 4 generations now since 1941. We have established our brand in the market by providing high quality, pure and artistic jewelry items since inception.

BH Store specializes in all types of gemstones including Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald, Cat’s eye, Diamond, Coral, Hessonite (Gomed), Pearl’s and provide them at wholesale price. We also procure our superior gemstones from Kashmir, Burma, Srilanka, Colombia as we travel to mines worldwide to locate and acquire the finest that is available in the market. We are one of the leading exporters and importers to USA, Europe and Asia.

Backed with proven knowledge of gemstones and manufacturing, we are planning to expand our business on global level through www.bhstore.in. We constantly strive to make sure that you enjoy an easy, fast, safe and secure shopping experience online.

We are promoted by professionals who know the diamond jewellery business inside out as well as one of the pioneers of the online industry in India. Like all online businesses, we derive strength from having low overheads and low costs. In addition, we do our own manufacturing and are not just a re-seller like most online businesses. We have a dedicated call centre to help customers. Feel free to call us and share your requirement.
