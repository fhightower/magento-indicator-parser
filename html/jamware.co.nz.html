Automation Product Distributors

JAMware Automation Solutions take full advantage of the network connectivity of modern equipment to implement a control system by talking directly to network devices, removing the need for traditional, expensive control hardware.

The Automation Solutions will integrate simply and are not restricted to any one brand of tablet, smart phone or other consumer device.
