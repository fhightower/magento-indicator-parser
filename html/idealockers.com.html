Custom Lockers by Ideal Products

Our quality lockers are 100% made in the U.S.A. Over 30 years of research and development has resulted in Ideal Products being a top choice for locker room products and designs for varying industries and architects.

Wood Lockers

Choose from solid natural wood or wood veneer in multiple configurations, from single tier up to ten tier, and our popular z-locker style (Models L and M). Custom locker sizes are also available. Ideal for colleges and schools, athletics, as well as classy country clubs. Wood Lockers >>

Plastic Laminate Lockers

Fire retardent and durable lockers available with countless finishes to match your branding and facility colors/textures. Available in numerous storage configurations including single tier, double tier, z-locker executive sllye double tiers (Models L and M), triple tier, all the way up to 10 tier. Ideal for fitness centers, medical centers, military, and more. Plastic Laminate Lockers >>

Locker Room Amenities

We also offer a variety of locker room benches, locker room stools, and grooming stations to complete your locker room design and add that special final touch and comfort. Available in a variety of materials and finishes to compliment and match your custom lockers. Locker Room Amenities >>

Locks for Lockers

Give your lockers added security with our variety of locks - combination locks, coin collect, coin return, RFID, keycard locks, multi-user, user programmable, padlock hasps, and touch keypad locks. Locker Locks >>
