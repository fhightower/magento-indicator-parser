 About Us

Whether you are watching your favorite sports team, rooting for your college, or dancing at a club, Electro Flash Media t-shirts are guaranteed to make the room shine! Our t-shirts are made with Electroluminescence Technology, which allows our product to light up when activated by sound. If it’s your favorite band playing or the sound of your friends cheering next to you this t-shirt will be sure to electrify the crowd.

Electro Flash Media Commercial from Chase Wright on Vimeo.
