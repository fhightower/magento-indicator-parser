Welcome Wine Lovers!

SterlingWineOnline.com began with our desire to offer a unique selection of wine-themed products after living in California on the outskirts of wine country. We have been all about wine ever since! Wine lover gifts are our specialty, whether you’re treating yourself or a fellow wine lover!

 

A wine country basket is always in style and is a simple choice for anyone who enjoys food and wine. Looking for something more special? Choose from a selection of personalized wine gifts and add a name or message for a thoughtful touch. Have environmentally friendly friends on your list? We have plenty of eco wine products to choose from. We even have wine gifts for men. What’s better than custom barrels that can dispense wine straight into your glasses? Serve your wine in style! Whatever you’re looking for, we have all the best gifts for wine lovers!

Looking for something for yourself? Browse our selection of wine décor including wine barrel furniture and eco wine furniture or choose one of our unique wine racks, all of which will add character to your home and wow your guests! We also carry a huge selection of other wine décor including wall art, tapestries, clocks, candles, and more. Not quite at your level of wine loving yet? Want to wear your wine? Find amazing wine apparel and accessories as well! We have everything from wine shirts and hats to ties and even a wine umbrella!

We know that wine goes well with other things, that’s why we also offer cigar humidor barrel kits, travel bags, cigar box purses, wine ornaments, and even wine related products for pets. We hope that our love of wine shows in the quality and uniqueness of our products. We know you’ll find the perfect gift for yourself or any wine lover in your life!
