Our products for Biofeedback, Neurofeedback, AVS and more

We offer articles related to all aspects of Biofeedback, Neurofeedback, and audiovisual stimulation at Mindfield-shop.com. You will find devices for both professional and home use. To complement these items, you will find a range of accessories such as electrodes, pastes, gels, hoods, caps, cables, sensors, and much more. All articles are optimally compatible with each other.

Our various software packages and modules offer a wide palette of application possibilities for professional Biofeedback and Neurofeedback training.

In order to make Biofeedback and Neurofeedback procedures more widely known, we offer a series of flyers and posters on various topics. These are professionally made by an advertising agency; they have a unified look and are written in easily understandable language. You are welcome to use them for further advertising purposes.
