How we are able to sell replacement keyless remote Key fobs for such a large discount?

 

     99% of our key & remote products are re-manufactured back to “like new” condition or brand new. Each keyless remote is taken apart by our professional technicians so they are able to test the OEM circuit board(s) with diagnostic equipment to ensure perfect functionality. After the testing is complete and the keyless remote board(s) are re-shelled with new buttons and brand new batteries, each replacement keyless entry remote is tested 3 times:

 
1. After the keyless remote re-manufactured assembly
2. Before being added to our product inventory stock room
3. Prior to being packaged and shipped to our customers

Since we do not have every car currently in existence in the USA to test these small cases we are sending the item to you in faith that the item is perfect.
