With over 35 years experience in the Pro Audio and Entertainment industry Wireless Sounds is your Comprehensive RF, Audio & Lighting Solution. Everyone on staff is trained and certified to design, install, and operate everything utilized for your application. Our team will help every step of the way making your project, event or purchase the success it deserves to be.

Our Services

PRODUCTION

Pro Audio, Lighting, Staging and Backline equipment rentals and crew to make it happen.

SYSTEM INTEGRATION

Permanent lighting, sound, and video system installations. Our team of highly trained and certified professionals can execute all disciplines of system design and engineering.

SERVICE

Whether your gear requires repair or simply some routine maintenance, our service department is full of the most capable and talented service technicians in the industry.

RENTALS

We carry a vast inventory of gear, accessories, and expendables from top manufacturers.

SALES

We carry most of the items we use everyday for sale in new and used conditions. Used items are kept in pristine condition as our engineers and system technicians test and prepare these items to the highest standards.
