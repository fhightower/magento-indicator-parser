The design of the Elevator bridle came from a horse of the same name owned by qualified saddle fitter Lorraine Green. This revolutionary bridle is the ORIGINAL comfort bridle that has gone on to set the standard for many bridle designs today. Produced in the UK using only the highest premium English leather, the Elevator bridle is often imitated but NEVER equalled!! Elevator has now brought out numerous new bridle ranges as well as many more amazing new products so please enjoy browsing the site.

DID YOU KNOW??

Elevator offers a mix and match service so if you require different size parts to make up your bridle we can assist with this.

All Elevator bridles are available in a range of sizes and colours so if you can not see you preferred size or colour, or you require our mix and match service, please contact the office and we will be happy to help in anyway possible.

For a small additional charge all Elevator bridles are also available from our made-to-measure service – so no matter what size your horse or pony is, there is an Elevator bridle with their name on it!
