Polymini produce hand made dolls house miniatures in 1/12th, 1/24th & 1/48th scale, mainly in polymer clay but also in wood and other materials. We also have a range of equipment to help you produce your own miniatures in polymer clay. As all items are individually hand made some variation in colour and form will occur from that depicted within these pages and the catalogue.

For some items we can alter or make them to your own requirements, i.e. wedding cakes, tea trolley, in that colours can be specified. Use the contact us form for your queries and/or requirements, and we will contact you to discuss things.
