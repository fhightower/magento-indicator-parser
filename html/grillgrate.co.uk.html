Now you don't need to buy the latest hi-tech gas barbecue or the latest charcoal kettle barbecue to achieve infra red performance or cook amazing food. Just upgrade your bbq cooking grids / bbq cooking grates to GrillGrate to get hi-tech low cost infra red performance. GrillGrates fit absolute any bbq as a bbq cooking grate accessory and for many UK barbecues there is a GrillGrate to replace your existing cooking grids. Instead of buying a new gas barbecue or a new charcoal barbecue - upgrade your cooking grids with GrillGrate or use GrilGrate as replacement cooking grid.

GrillGrate is your easy route to great tasting, perfectly cooked delicious food on any barbecue! It will even work on your DIY store own brand barbecues, to find the correct GrillGrate for your barbecue simply measure your existing cooking grids / grates or choose a size slightly smaller to use as an accessory on top of your bbq. Alternatively visit our A-Z BBQ library to find out which GrillGrate fits your grill best and whether you can replace your cooking grids with GrillGrate or use GrillGrate as an accessory - either way it works!

If you have a Weber BBQ that your require replacement cooking grates / grids - you can use GrillGrate, Weber Gas barbecues cook better food with GrilGrate! Weber charcoal barbecues cook better with GrillGrate. If you have Napoleon BBQ, Outback BBQ or even a Swiss Grill barbecue and you want to cook better food - just use GrillGrate. GrillGrate even works with Kamado Joe's, Big Green Egg, Cadac Barbecues, Grand Hall Barbecues, Broil King Barbecues, Beefeater Barbecues, Char Broil Barbecues, Landmann, Grillstream, Leisuregrow, Dancook and even Buschbeck Barbecues.
