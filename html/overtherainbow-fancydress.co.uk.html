WELCOME TO OVER THE RAINBOW FANCY DRESS

Over the Rainbow specialise in selling and hiring good quality fancy dress costumes and accessories, direct in our shop in Pudsey, Leeds West Yorkshire and through the internet via our online fancydress store.

We stock a large selection of Adult Fancy Dress including; Adult Where’s Wally costume, Pirate fancy dress, 80’s fancy dress, Clown fancy dress, 70’s fancy dress, Army fancy dress, Halloween costumes and New years fancydress. To accompany our dressing up selection we have a large number of accessories and wigs, including: Afro wig, 70’s wig, Cowboy accessories, Fishnet tights, Neon tutus, Fake blood, Liquid latex and Snazaroo face paints.

We also stock children’s costumes for world book day and curriculum topics including Romans / Victorians and Tudors. For those special occasions, nights out and parties we also supply helium balloons, handmade fascinators and handmade jewellery.

Come and see us soon, there is ample free parking to the front and rear of the shop.
