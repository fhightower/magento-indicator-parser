Welcome to our online store

Micronit Microfluidics has more than 15 years experience in Developing, Prototyping and Manufacturing Lab-On-A-Chip Products.
We focus on Organ-on-a-chip, Droplets, Enhanced Oil Recovery, Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy, Flowcells & Observation, Micromixers, Microreactors and Capillary Electrophoresis.  We deliver to customers all around the world and stand for Quality, Innovation and Volume.
We invite you to take a look and browse our online store, so you can have a look into the world of Microfluidics!
