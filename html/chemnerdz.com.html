ChemNerdz liquid nicotine is completely organic and derived from tobacco leaves in an FDA registered and inspected facility. After the extraction process, laboratory technicians test the purity of the nicotine on a GC/MS. These test results are analyzed, and if the purity of the nicotine is 99.9% or higher, the nicotine is given a batch number, and the results are compiled to make the Certificate of Analysis. This Pure Nicotine Certificate of Analysis is included in the package with every nicotine order. Rest assured all of our nicotine solutions exceed the United States Pharmacopoeia standards.

ChemNerdz sells the highest quality Vegetable Glycerin – VG – available! Vegetable Glycerin USP Kosher is a key ingredient that is used to dilute liquid nicotine for E-Liquid or E-juices. Vegetable Glycerin plays two important roles in e-liquid and e-juices, it provides a thick, luscious cloud of vapor and a light sweet taste!

ChemNerdz sells the highest quality Propylene Glycol – PG- available! Propylene Glycol USP Kosher is a key ingredient that is used to dilute liquid nicotine for E-Liquid or E-juices. Propylene Glycol is a tasteless and odorless substance that is relatively thin in consistency. Because of this consistency it is more easily absorbed by the polyfill fabric inside the cartomizer of the electronic cigarette, does not alter the flavor of your e-liquid in any way, and provides the throat hit that smokers are looking for, similar to tobacco.
