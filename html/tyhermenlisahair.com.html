Remy hair extensions commonly refer to those extensions type with the characteristics of uni-lateral cuticle direction, saying all hair follicles go in the single direction. These remy hair deliver you the kind of feeling, e.g soft, silky smooth, while you are touching it with hands.Although you could not distinguish the difference between remy and non remy only with eyes, some sellers claim their "remy" hair with very low price, normally, they are non remy. 100% remy hair extensions are in quality and also price high. Those low priced so called "remy" hair extensions start to matte up only after around half of months to 20 days. Premium remy hair extensions normally last up to 3 to 6 months if you maintain them regularly.
