Product Highlight: Recombinant Trypsin

Richcore products Rc TrypsinTM and Rc TrypsinTM (Powder) are 100 % animal origin free replacements for bovine and porcine trypsin. Both products are produced in Richcore's GMP certified facility by recombinant DNA technology using Pichia pastoris host sytem and an artificially synthesized gene of trypsin
Contract Manufacturing

Medium and large scale manufacturing of recombinant proteins and enzymes in our GMP certified, state of the art upstream and ISO Class 8 downstream facility.

Research and Development

Development of recombinant proteins and enzymes through our expertise in Molecular Biology, Method and Process Development, Small Scale Manufacturing, and Analytical profiling of proteins. Multiple platforms available, including P. pastoris, K. lactis, B. subtilis, and E. coli.
