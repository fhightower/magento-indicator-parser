Washroom Equipment News

Bobrick has been serving the commercial washroom equipment market since 1906. Bobrick is known as the premier brand in washroom accessores and restroom partitons. With locations in Los Angeles, Memphis and New York we can offer you fast and affordable shipping rates. Shipping rates are calculated based on the closest warehouse to your delivery address. We have been a Bobrick authorized distributor since 1988. Our friendly knowledgeable staff is here to help if you should have any questions.

American Specialties offers superior design and the most extensive range of washroom equipment  products in the industry. Since 1961, ASI has provided thousands of products that have satisfied the most discerning designers and given our customers the peace of mind, that, no matter what they build, ASI will have products that will stand the test of time. Architects, designers, building owners, facility managers and construction professionals have placed their trust in ASI products for some of the most prestigious projects throughout the world for decades.
Accurate Partitions Corp. has manufactured toilet partitions since 1957. While many things have changed during that time, our commitment to value and quality remains steadfast.The Accurate line of powder coated steel, stainless steel, solid plastic, phenolic and plastic laminate partitions represents one of the most versatile single sources of toilet partitions available today. Accurate pioneered the use of powder coatings on metal partitions, now the industry standard. This revolutionary development improved the air quality of our environment by eliminating air-borne solvents. The powder coat process also produces a stronger, more durable, finish.
