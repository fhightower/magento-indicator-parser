About Craft Holsters

Wide Selection of Concealed Carry Holsters


Craft Holsters is a company specializing in the distribution of top quality concealed carry holsters and duty gear all over the world. Our aim is to bring premium products from traditional European manufacturers to American customers. Duty and concealed carry holsters is a growing market and with years in business we offer a wide selection of holsters from leading producers Falco and Vega, known for their quality materials and custom hand crafted manufacture. We stock about 1000 products to ensure quick order handling and we cooperate with leading shipping companies to offer fast, reliable and price effective delivery worldwide. We are working hard to satisfy your holster needs and we are offering new innovative models of concealed carry holsters based on years of experience in professional (Police, Military, Spec Ops, Law Enforcement) and regular gun wearing.
