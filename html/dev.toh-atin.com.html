A One-Of-A-Kind Native American Art Gallery

To see a great selection of Native American art, visit the Toh-Atin Gallery in Durango, CO. Our Native American art gallery has grown to be one of the most respected art galleries in North America, and we have a very generous inventory of Navajo Indian rugs, Navajo weavings, Native American/Indian jewelry, turquoise jewelry, and Indian Art. Visitors to our gallery can count on our knowledgeable staff to help them learn about our displays and choose the perfect piece of art for their home.

We are an art gallery that has a deep experience trading in authentic Native American art. The antiques on offer, whether Pueblo pottery, Kachina, Native American baskets, Sculptures, bronzes or masks, are carefully selected to ensure they are of the highest quality possible. As a member of the Indian Arts and Crafts Association and the Antique Tribal Arts Dealers Association, you can be sure that we have developed a national reputation for have quality merchandise and great relationships with both artists and long-term collectors.

There's always something new to explore at our Native American art gallery. We host events all over the southwest and welcome tourists from Cortez, CO and across the country. Visit us today!
