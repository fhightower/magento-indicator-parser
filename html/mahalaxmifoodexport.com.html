WEL COME TO MAHALAXMI FOOD EXPORT

Conceived by Founder Mr. Amit Jaiswal, Mahalaxmi Food Export is an enterprise which established its base in 2008. Since its inception, Mahalaxmi Food Export has been to procure agricultural products par excellence directly from Global Gap Certified farms. We adhere to strict quality compliance and have created a niche for ourselves in the realms of fresh Spices exports, Oil Seeds as well as exports of numerous Indian Grains.
We are propelled by a strong ethical and value based ideology and with this we surpass the expectations of all our esteemed customers and clients who are based around the globe. We are registered under govt authorities such as KAPPEC, APEDA, MINISTRY OF COMMERCE, CENTRAL SALES TAX, and SPICE BOARD.

The Spices and Oil Seeds exported by us undergo a rigorous quality analysis and only then are they deemed fit to be exported to the clients. We are known for our prompt services and wide network which makes it easy for us to deliver the agricultural products within the stipulated timeframe to ensure that their shelf life is not affected by changes in the climate and other environmental factors.

Our sole aim is to provide ace grade products to the clients at affordable prices and we constantly work for fulfilling our commitments. We are motivated by a sense of accomplishment when the clients are satisfied with our products and have high regard for our professional commitment. This is what makes Mahalaxmi Food Export feature in the world map of exporters.

Why “MLFE”

With our rich technological expertise and years of experience, we have attained an indispensable position in the global market. We are one of the renowned and preferred names in the food processing industry engaged in processing, supplying & exporting a wide range of Indian spices.

  • Certificate of Inspection issued by SGS India Ltd

  • The company recorded 25% growth rate in the last four years
  • All containers factory stuffed, SGS inspection & analysis certificates with each and every shipment
