Welcome to Coir Matting UK

Coir mats are one of the most useful and tough form of entrance matting which is known for its toughness, hard-wearing and water absorption qualities. This makes them an ideal choice for all types of entrances like at bathroom doorway, pool side entrances hall-ways and main entrance. Coconut coir is used for the manufacturing of industrial coir mats and commercial coir mats all over the word. Coir entrance mats UK are available in different shades of brown which perfectly match your surrounding environment.
