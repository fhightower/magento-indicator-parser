Jewellers Melbourne | Hearts On Fire | Ole Lynggaard Copenhagen

Trewarne Fine Jewellery is a family jewellery business with over 80 years of experience . We specialise in diamond engagement rings and wedding rings in Melbourne, Australia.

Our three stores across the city are premier stockists for main brands Hearts On Fire and Ole Lynggaard Copenhagen. We can also manufacture our own bespoke jewellery

designs and stock a full range of earrings, necklaces, pendants, bracelets and other associated diamond, gold and silver jewellery.
