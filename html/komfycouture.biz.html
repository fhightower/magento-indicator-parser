Welcome to Komfy Couture

We are a family-owned, online retail clothing store specializing in soft, comfortable clothing that not only feels good, but also looks good. As parents of two children with sensory processing disorder/tactile defensiveness, we have struggled through the years to find comfortable clothing that is also fashionable. This is where we were inspired to create a line of clothing where comfort and fashion collide.
