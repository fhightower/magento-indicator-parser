    About Us

    Located five km's from Brisbane CBD, When Freddie met Lilly has expanded its range from casual, cocktail & formal dresses to bridal gowns over the past 5 - 6 years. We are renowned to have the most stunning gowns available in Brisbane. Plus our Bridesmaid collections are incredible.
     
    Our own house label - When Freddie met Lilly, is available in a few hand picked boutiques across Australia. The collection ranges from Bridesmaid gowns through to Bridal Gowns and Cocktail Dresses. Changing with seasons, each collection is growing and evolving.
