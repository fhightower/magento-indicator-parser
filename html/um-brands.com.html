  • UM! Brands uses a new technology called EverSTIKTM Nanosuction to create peel and stick products that adhere to vertical surfaces without tools and without damage. Using this technology, we’ve created wall hooks, sticky pads, portable whiteboards, clips, mirrors and picture frames that are all portable and reusable. EverSTIKTM works on most household surfaces including wood, tile, glass, stainless steel, mirrors, and smooth concrete. With EverSTIKTM , you can say goodbye to glue, tape, and nails.

  • UM! Brands™ has surpassed the impossible with our patented EverSTIK™ material. Using a thermoplastic elastomer imbued with our proprietary formula, we are able to create what can be best described as the effect of 'nano-suction.’

    In its simplest terms, nano-suction is the power of millions of microscopic suction cups, packed as tightly as the cells of your skin that it feels like a smooth plastic surface to your hand.
