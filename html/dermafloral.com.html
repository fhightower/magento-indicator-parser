Welcome Dermafloral

In today's fast-paced environment, stress from the rat race has upset the balance of nature, leading to serious skin problems like acne breakouts and dehydration. In view of these problems and the needs of modern women, Beaux Ex Bellus Trading was incorporated in February 2001. The company’s unique treatments and high quality products received positive response from customers and in December 2002, the company expanded to set up Derma Floral Beauty Pte Ltd. Despite the proliferation of similar beauty start-ups in recent years, our company has enjoyed continued support and loyalty from satisfied customers.. .
