Organic Baby Clothing is the safest choice for your babies

The material from the clothing that your baby wears or anything they come into contact with for long periods of time can slowly enter their bodies through their pores. This includes their blankets, clothing and including diapers. Our line of organic baby wear is the safest choice and you can rest easy while knowing they are safe. What are the benefit of Organic Baby Wear?

It's no secret that babies skin are much more sensitive compared to adults. Thus the reason why we put lotions, powders and vaseline in an effort to protect their skin from harm as much as possible. Parents need to know that clothing has a continued impact on respiratory system and development. The impact is greater on babies, due to their sensitive skin. This is why choosing the right clothing, diapers, blankets and pretty much everything to do with your baby is very vital. Especially when non-organic fibers are often treated with pesticides, many experts (and parents) choose organic baby clothes as the only way to go.

Think all cotton is treated and grown the same? Think again

Billions of dollars' worth of pesticides are sprayed daily on cotton fields every year on a continuous basis. It is widely known that five of the top nine pesticides used on cotton are cancer-causing chemicals.
