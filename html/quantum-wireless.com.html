  • About Us
 

Comprised of a dedicated group of mobile communications experts, Quantum is committed to availing their customers of cutting-edge products at the lowest market prices. Part of the Quantum credo is the deeply-embedded belief that personable and efficient customer service is key to lasting product satisfaction.

With a company culture fueled by intuition, creativity, innovation and technical expertise, the Quantum team channels these attributes into its strategic vision, business practices and all customer interactions. We take great pride in our products, and we are confident that our customers will appreciate the exceptional quality of Quantum products.

Our high quality Cell Phone Signal Boosters and Networking Accessories (Routers, Access Points, Wireless Bridges...) have brought many businesses and families more reliable cellphone reception and faster internet connectivity. The leading products in this line include America's best-selling signal booster, the YX545 Dual Band Amplifier,, 4G Signal Boosters from Wilson or Cellphone-Mate or Cloud Managed Access Points from Meraki.

Through its unique sales-assist model, one-ring phone service and multi-channel accessibility, Quantum translates your needs into customized solutions. Our price-match policy guarantees that your orders are acquired at a price unmatched by any other authorized online dealer. Should it occur that you find a price better than ours, please feel free to call us at 1-877-259-4629. One of our friendly and knowledgeable sales representatives will be happy to assist you Monday through Friday between 9AM and 5 PM EST.
