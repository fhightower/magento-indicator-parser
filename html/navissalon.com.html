Welcome to Navis Salon!

Navis Hair & Beauty Salon is a one stop Beauty and grooming solution for both Women and Men. We specialize in both traditional yet modern hair & beauty services and treatment. Our main service includes Threading, Waxing, henna hair treatment and most recently the popular IPL treatment.

We have been known to provide the best facial service, one of the only known Indian salon using premium quality products that shows results.

At the Navis, we believe in beauty with a conscience. We have created a salon that offers the highest quality hair services in a setting that is healthier for the environment, our guests and our staff. We are committed to providing healthy hair care with a low impact on the environment. We believe in natural therapeutic treatment that brings in the inner glow from within

We operate our business from our Holland V branch dedicated only on for female clients and a unisex hair & beauty salon at 92A Syed Alwi Road.
