Ken Rand Furniture - Design and supply of quality commercial furniture


We carefully select and supply high-end furniture solutions. Our excellent range of furniture covers healthcare, educational, retail and corporate environments.

If you need advice about designing your space, our experienced design team can help. We offer a FREE interior design service and specialise in bespoke furniture design whether it’s a new product or customising an existing product to suit your requirements. We offer a professional spacial consultation and can produce 3D visuals to show the furniture in situ.

New projects mean new challenges. We love the challenge of designing a single piece of furniture, a bespoke reception, or the design and refurbishment of a complete area. We are always open to new projects and ideas.

We invite you to take a look at our on-line products. No matter if you are searching for an office chair, meeting pod, reception desk, pharmacy shelving, boardroom furniture, you will find it on our site. If however you can’t see what you are looking for, give us a call. We will do our best to source the product for you.
