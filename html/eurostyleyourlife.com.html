«eurostyle your life» is a message and a promise at the same time. We are convinced that valuable products are produced only when form (design), material, quality workmanship, functionality and price are in a complementary unison with one another. We are searching the European market for suppliers who work according to this principle. We want to offer new and innovative designers from Europe a platform to present their new design and product ideas to the American public.
