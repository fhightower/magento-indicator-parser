Put a smile on someone’s face today with a beautiful gift from Hampers Art. Hamper Art is a Canberra based business designing and creating gifts, hampers, baskets and boxes for all occasions. We pride ourselves on Style and Sophistication; we add a personal touch to all our gifts and Hampers.

As Canberra’s luxury Hamper and Basket service, we do whatever it takes to impress our clients. We don’t believe in using cheap imports to keep costs down, we believe in making fabulous luxury hampers with the best local and Australian products. Where possible we use Canberra based products and produce, this in turn supports Canberra’s local industries and community and showcases the best our region has to offer. Our Canberra range of gift baskets, gourmet hampers and gifts is what we are renowned for. Our local Canberra Gourmet Hampers are in growing demand and very popular, local produce and gourmet products are not only very fresh but they help the environment in a number of ways.

Gift giving and hamper creation is what we are passionate for, whether it be a gourmet hamper with some of Canberra’s best foods and wine, a beautiful wrapped baby basket or a beer hamper in a box, then we will take care of it. Every gift is presented in a modern sophisticated way, delivering it with a smile and making someone’s day.

We offer same day delivery to Canberra and Queanbeyan and all our hampers, gifts and baskets can be delivered Australia wide. Next day delivery available to all Capital cities. Why not send a unique Canberra hamper as a gift to someone special?

Our most popular hampers a certainly our Gourmet Canberra Baskets and boxes. We use many different gourmet products including: Gourmet Taste Bud jams and sauces, Lindsay & Edmunds Chocolates - all handmade here in Canberra, Real Chai is very yummy and very Canberra, Batemans Bay nut roasting company do some fabulous gourmet nuts, Shaw wines are from Murrumbateman, just near Canberra and they make some of the finest wine around, Serious Salsa & Sauces make great gourmet salsa, sauces and more. These are just some of the Canberra items you will find in our gourmet baskets and hampers.

Looking for a baby basket, gourmet hamper, food and wine gift or beer tub? We can help. We can also custom all our hampers and baskets. If you are looking for a special gift and you can’t see it on our site please call us and we can specially design the perfect gift, hamper, box or basket for your special person.

Please contact us if you are after something corporate, this is another one of our specialty areas. Branding a hamper box or gift basket is something we can do, if you have specific ideas or needs just have a chat with us and we can design you the perfect corporate gift, Canberra hampers or gift basket.
