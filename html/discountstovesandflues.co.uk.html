  Welcome to Discount Stoves and Flues

At Discount Stoves and Flues we supply a range of multi-fuel, wood burning and DEFRA approved stoves together with a variety of flue products, including twin wall flexible flue liner, components, cowls, and accessories, and a selection of installation kits; all at extremely competitive prices.

We pride ourselves in providing excellent customer service together with high quality products. Our multi-fuel flue liners are both CE and HETAS approved, supplied with a 10 year warranty and delivered to your door the next working day (UK Mainland only).   More >
