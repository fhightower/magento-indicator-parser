Eshish Electronic Shisha: Your Tobacco Free E Cigarette

 

Eshishin is your one-stop shop for all your vaping needs. Wether you need E-liquids, Electronic Cigarettes or accessories, Eshishin has what you need. With a quick delivery time, you are sure to get what you need when you need it. 

People all over the UK have been raving about the benefits of vaping and it’s not hard to see why. It’s cheap and a much healthier alternative, For those who are currently smoking, Eshishin provides E-liquids ranging from low levels of nicotine (0-8mg per bottle) to much higher levels (16-24mg). Whatever your current smoking habit is, we can provide an easy, affordable and far healthier solution to regular cigarettes.

With so many parts, kits and types of E-cigarettes it can be difficult to know where to start. With Eshishin you can start with shisha sticks, small disposable e-cigarettes available in a range of colours and flavours with a design reminiscent of a standard tobacco filled cigarette, these nifty little devices last for about four to five hundred puffs and are available for a low price.

If you are already an experienced vaper and you want to step up your game, you can go from having a standard shisha pen to a 650, 900 or 1100 with each providing more vapour per pull. If you’re indecisive and don’t know which battery size to go for, don’t fear because you can also get an EGO Twist battery, which allows you to change the power of the battery to suit your needs. We here at Eshishin are here to make sure you get what you need.

If you fancy a change from the standard Tobacco flavour liquids we can also help with that too! We have Cherry, Mint, Strawberry, Banana, Chocolate and many other flavour liquids for you to fill your E-cigarette with.

If you have a flair for the dramatic, you can also get E-cigarettes that act and work like a pipe. If you want a larger battery for your existing clearomiser (That’s the bit you put the liquid in) then you can get those separately too. Do you need a case for your E Cigarette when you’re travelling? We have those too. Lanyards to carry it? Check. Chargers in case yours develops a fault? Check. New tanks, sold separately on their own if you need a replacement or are worried that the flavour of your old liquid will remain in the tank when you go to fill it with a new flavour? Check!

Eshishin is a top company, based around providing you with the means to kick your smoking habit to the curb, or continue smoking with no regrets. It’s your choice and we’re here to help! So stop reading, start browsing today!
