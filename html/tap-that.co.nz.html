A warning about older and slower Tablet Computers
 
Some other retailers are selling lower specification, lower quality tablets at the more affordable prices. Which might be okay, if they cheap tablets for kids. But experience shows me that anything less than about a Quad Core CPU, with 2GB of RAM (slightly different for Windows, and octacore with 1GB will also perform good enough), will most likely run very slow sometimes. You might ask, well, does that matter? And my answer is yes, it really does. 
 
If your touch device is running slower than a dusty old PC, it ruins the experience, makes the device much less useful. If you buy a tablet that is less than these specifications, you may well regret you spent the money at all! So beware of lower quality or older technology, even if it comes with a well known brand name.
 
That's why we provide a middle ground, cheaper tablets that are decent, modern devices.

Cheap Tablets, but decent Modern Specifications

And we reckon that's actually too expensive for what you get. And we also believe that you shouldn't have to be rich, to have access to easy convenient, bigger screen computing. 
 
That's why we strive to find the best quality to price ratio, and source great tablets and accessories that people can more easily afford! :)
