The Palm Store by Surf-Lines

The Palm Store stocks the full range of Palm Equipment's canoe and kayak products, including the latest technical clothing, footwear and safety gear. For adventurous paddlers of any age and ability we have the latest whitewater, touring, sea and crossover kayaks by Dagger and Wilderness Systems. For a relaxing, easy to learn and enormously fun kayak check out our range of sit-on-top kayaks from Wilderness Systems and Islander or a top quality inflatable kayak by Infinity. Or for a traditional open canoeing experience we have the range of Mad River canoes in hard-wearing Triple Tough material or lightweight and durable Royalex
