About us

An exhibition is a bustling hub of activity where new ideas can be exchanged and innovations are brought to life. Like any activity, they rely on sufficient amount of facilitations, strategic planning, and composition to help make them successful. Vivid Ads is a skilful, professional, and discount retailer of a wide range of products used for tradeshows. We are located in Port of Melbourne with a massive showroom and production facility.

Vivid Ads business structure is more sophisticated than simply selling you a product. There are many more models of planning and execution that can benefit from having an experienced team assisting. Efficient planning is an all meaningful task in the execution of any major event whether hosting or a participant. Vivid Ads team aims to provide that much needed experience and ensures that whatever a person’s tradeshow targets are, they will all be accomplished.
