InFoPaK International manufactures Award Winning outdoor and indoor literature holders, dispensers and displays to aid in the distribution of marketing literature and other pertinent information.

Utilizing a unique, patented manufacturing process (100% Made In USA), our line of "Eco-Friendly" products are engineered to be weather resistant, see-thru, versatile and best of all... inexpensive! It seems the critics agree: The Society of Plastic Engineers has awarded the honor of "Top promotional product design" to the InFoPaK JR.

InFoPaK's products are found in many different industries (Point-Of-Purchase, real estate, restaurant, automobile, advertising, nursery, marine, etc.) and in over eight countries.
