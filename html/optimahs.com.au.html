Welcome!

As you can imagine, being #1 in Australia for Dental Practice Design and Construction means we've heard and seen it all. You see, for more than 25 years Optima Healthcare Group has been providing everything you need to stay relevant in the market, and overcome the challenges you face in your daily routine with full clinic construction and transformation.

The Result: We're in tune with your industry in a way that no other dental surgical supplies or healthcare supply solutions provider can claim.

Now you can leverage the same expertise that more than 25 years of excellence has delivered to more than 300 of your dental and healthcare colleagues with our simple to use 'gap-eliminating' solution.

One of our energetic and friendly consultants will quickly respond to serve you in every corner of Australia including Victoria, New South Wales, Queensland, South Australia, Western Australia, ACT, Tasmania and the Northern Territory.
