Decorate, Add Privacy To Glass Doors

Decorate doors with your art, logo & photos, print-ready art, or customize one of DecoStat's 60+ designs.

 

Pricing is based on size of film ordered (# of sq. inches).  Select type of window or door being covered. Enter glass measurements for pricing to be calculated and to begin customizing. (If you want to start before you have measured, guess-timate the size, glass measurements can be changed later.)
 
All prints are produced with DecoStat's 'Giclee' print technology for glass. Artwork will have the beautiful colors and qualities of decorative glass.

Designs are printed on DecoStat's adhesive-free clear or privacy film.   Recommended for windows, glass doors, shower enclosures, mirrors, etc.
