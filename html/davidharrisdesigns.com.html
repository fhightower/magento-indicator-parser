About David Harris

David is the type of artist who maintains a specifically green ideal with his art. He re-purposes the material to create new works.
His kind of creativity and ecological sensitivity is rare in the world of luxury goods, and sets David Harris apart as an artist and designer who holds the natural world in high esteem and as a source of true inspiration.
