Trade Fair International started out trading within the Antiques Markets in England, Paris, Belgium and Budapest and based on the knowledge and experience gained, we have since grown and evolved over the last 25 years into selling traditional to Contemporary Furniture and design led furniture and gifts to the Trade. Our wide and diverse range of stock includes over 6000 unique products, from mirrors and furniture to lighting and accessories and gifts, from classical, traditional styles to more modern and quirky pieces.

We welcome all Trade customers to our showroom where many of our 6000 products can be viewed Trade Fair International is based in Lincoln and we offer a cash and carry service.
We are Open  8.30 - 4.00  Monday  - Friday
