Important Customer Information

Delivery

All products will be delivered by courier to your door. The Delivery cost is FREE unless specified during the order process. Delivery will take less than one week unless otherwise specified. We deliver to mainland UK only at present.

Important Customer Information

Availability

If a bike is out of stock we will not sell it to you. You can be confident that the bike you order is in stock as those out of stock are removed from the website on a regular basis. In the unlikely event that we cannot get the bike from the supplier you will be notified and the order will be cancelled.

Find out more...
Important Customer Information

The Product

You will receive the bike(s) 95% built. Bikes are packaged to save space and protect them from damage. On receipt of the bike you will have to remove packaging; attach seat; handlebars; wheels and pedals. The extent of building you will have to do is slightly dependent upon the bike but should take approximately 20 minutes. All bikes come with the appropriate instructions and mini tool kit.

Important Customer Information

Refund Policy

You, the consumer, have the right to cancel your order within 14 days of receipt of the goods. If you cancel your order, you will have your payment returned to you (these requirements comply with EU regulations). Please contact us by email with your order reference number and problem. We will arrange for collection by courier; after collection your card will be refunded the full amount.
