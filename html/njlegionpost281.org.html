2101 Chews Landing Rd. Blackwood, NJ 08012
Telephone: (856)-228-9869

 

 

 We want to take this time to Thank Veterans from all wars and conflicts. You men and Women have help keep American safe.

Thank you!

 
 
 
 

Post 281 is both a veterans' organization and a community resource. We provide a variety of services, programs and awards throughout the area. The officers and members of Stetser-Lamartine Post 281 would like to welcome you to our award-winning official website. Our post was chartered on June 8th, 1939,
and has grown considerably to include over 500 members we have today.

Additional programs are provided by our affiliated American Legion Auxiliary Unit 281, our Sons of the American Legion Squadron 281 and our Post Chapter 281 Legion Riders.

We will try and make your visit here both informative and enjoyable. Take some time and check out all of our categories to find out what is happening around our Post.

The catagories will also help you with most of your veteran-related questions on benefits and membership eligibility.

Thank you for stopping by!
