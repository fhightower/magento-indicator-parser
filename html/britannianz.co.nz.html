About Us

Britannia Textiles is one of New Zealand's leading importers, manufacturers, and distributors of linen and mats designed for laundry use.
 
Founded in 1987 by Nelson Laundry to source linen to its satisfaction, BT remains an independent family owned company. We wholesale under our own BT label; we don't have any retail outlets.
