Welcome to Gardener Gear - Quality Clothing for Keen Gardeners!

Here at Gardener Gear, we take great pride in offering the very best clothing and accessories for gardeners of all levels. We have years of experience supplying garden clothing across the UK and beyond, and strive to bring you the best quality at competitive prices, and great service too!

Whether you are a seasoned gardener, or just beginning to "dabble", it's important to wear the right protective clothing for the job. The right type of clothing can make gardening more enjoyable, reduce aches and pains and can significantly reduce the chance of injury.

Our range of gardening gear includes well respected brands such as Showa, 66 North, Sioen and Elka, offering a wide range of waterproof, thermal and long lasting clothing.
