About us

Esscential Candles are a family owned and run Australian business.

Created by a passionate Mother & Daughter duo from the Central Coast of New South Wales, our passion stemmed from our love of candles and the ability they have to spark a childhood memory, add a special touch to any occasion or to simply fill your home or space with an unforgettable aroma that will permeate for hours.

Our range is luxurious yet affordable and we are passionate about creating a memorable scent for our customers.

We are also extremely conscious about creating and maintaining an eco-friendly range. All our products are eco-friendly using only the highest quality non-toxic soy wax available and using natural lead-free cotton wicks. Our divine glass jars are also re-useable for everyday house hold items such as tea, coffee and sugar cannisters or for miscellaneous items in your bathroom.

We also offer custom made orders such as bonbonniere for your wedding guests. Our candles will make the perfect gift for your loved ones or if you simply feel like indulging yourself.

We hope you enjoy your Esscential Candles as much as we have loved developing this stunning range.
