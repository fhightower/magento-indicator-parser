about us

Nomadic Trading Company is a Durham NC based company, specializing in importing antique and decorative hand-made rugs, unique furniture, textiles, and home décor items from around the world. Since 1990 Nomadic has inspired the hospitality industry, designers as well our local community with handpicked vintage, repurposed and restored products.
