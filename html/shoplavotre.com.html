About The Shop

Lavotre (pronounced Lah-Vo-Tray) signifies "for you" in French. 

Thus complimenting our belief that every time piece chooses its owner.
Our commitment is in producing watches that are incessant, and suitable for everyday's #ootd.

Despite having a French word as our front, our designs are exquisitely Singapore. All designs are created locally and may often carry a touch of our melting pot of Singapore culture.

Our watches are running on Citizen Miyota Quartz Movement, Full leather straps, Water resistant to 3ATM, Enamel printed and comes with a year of warranty*. Should there be any defects, a one to one exchange would be given.

*Warranty only applies for Watch Faces and Watch Movements. 

Company Registration Number T14LL0946L
