Get to Know Our Collections

  

 

Organic Mattresses Manufacturer & Natural Bedding Design

At Naturepedic, safe and healthy sleep is our top priority. We specialize in the design and manufacturing of high-quality organic mattresses and all-natural bedding for infants, toddlers, children, and adults.

We believe that Mother Nature offers us the greatest materials for a good night’s sleep, and that adding in synthetic materials and harmful chemicals is both unnecessary and unhealthy. That’s why we exclusively design and manufacture organic mattresses, mattress pads and toppers, bedding, and more for every member of your family. It is our mission to partner with you in keeping dangerous chemicals and toxic substances out of your home.

All of our products, from our premium natural bedding to our state-of-the-art organic mattresses, are carefully crafted from completely natural and certified organic materials. They are non-toxic, fire-resistant, and are designed to promote and protect your family’s overall health and safety. Plus, they are incredibly comfortable and supportive, so you can rest well knowing that your family’s sleep environment is safe and free from harsh chemicals.

As a result of our rigorous quality standards, our partnerships with pediatricians, engineers, and chemists, and our product construction by Amish craftsmen, Naturepedic has become one of the premier organic mattress companies in the world.

You can find more information on Naturepedic’s revolutionary approach to organic mattress manufacturing here. Browse our wide range of organic mattresses, pads and toppers, and natural bedding. And don’t miss our organic bedding collections developed specifically for kids and babies.
