The Axia Office Advantage


Sharp, Kyocera, Oki & Fuji Xerox. Our choice of Brands is the most extensive available from a single photocopier supplier in Sydney and places us in a unique position to help you.

Our consultants understand the features and strengths of all our copier brands, and will take you through each one with impartiality and complete professionalism. This ensures you get a tailored solution to meet your copy, print and scan needs that will deliver significant savings over the lifetime of the multifunction copier and printers.

Our Managed Print Services offer purpose-developed software to continuously monitor all your print devices and ensure productivity is maximised. the cost of copier machines and printers, service and all consumables, including toner cartridges, are bundled together to provide a fixed cost per page. You'll be amazed by the savings you can make from day one.

Our fully trained service engineers are dedicated to delivering quick, reliable and proactive service. Our preventative maintenance program is designed to ensure your equipment lasts longer than you expect it to, delivering quality prints and copies all throughout its lifetime. Even more importantly, our engineers are passionate about their work and enjoy working here at Axia Office. In fact, most of them have been with us for over 12 years!
