Our Mission & Vision 

Mission

Inspect First™ aims to be the leading property inspection specialist in Singapore by offering affordable and reliable services that empower the homeowner. Our inspection reporting system protects our clients by providing an unbiased and accurate analysis of the condition of their properties. With our endorsement, our customers are given the peace of mind, knowing that their homes are in excellent condition.

 

Vision    

To continue to be a market leader and the most trusted property inspection specialist in Singapore.

 

The Benefits

  • CLAIMING OF LIABILITY

    In Singapore, new housing comes with a one-year Defects Liability Period (DLP). Contractors will use Inspect First™ reports to rectify defects.

  • COMPREHENSIVE REPORTS

    Inspect First™ comprises inspectors who have relevant certification and years of Building and Construction experience.

  • INDEPENDENT SERVICE

    Inspect First™ is the first organization in Singapore to provide home inspection services to the mass market. No conflict of interest.

  • LEGAL ASSISTANCE/ EXPERT WITNESS

    Inspect First™ provides you with credible evidence and support to settle legal issues that may arise.

  • OWNER'S PEACE OF MIND

    Inspect First™ guarantees that your property adheres to the local building and safety standards.
