About HealthyLifePlus
HealthyLifePlus was founded in 2014 and we are one of the fast developing health care focused shopping platform in Canada. We have noticed that Canadian customer are stucked with less selection and focused to pay more than Customers from United State. Our Goal is to change this situation by providing a better shopping platform for every Canadian Customers and vendor.  We will try our best to provide best selections of health related products and bring the price down to reasonable level which Canadian Customer deserves. That is how we would like to create the shopping experience in HealthyLifePlus. If you are a vendor who is interested in selling your product on our website, please do follow the instruction on the right hand side. We are really interested with cooperating with other suppliers. For any questions and suggestions, please do not hestitate to contact us at SUPPORT@HEALTHYLIFEPLUS.NET.
