Welcome to The Store at The Walt Disney Family Museum. We have chosen a few items to highlight our selection, which showcase Walt Disney, his vision, and the creations of those artists who pioneered the film, television, and Disney Parks that we enjoy today. To purchase items that are not available online, please visit the museum store located at The Walt Disney Family Museum at 104 Montgomery Street in the Presidio, San Francisco, California. No admission is required. Or give us a call directly at 415.345.6859. We're open every day from 10am-6pm, except Tuesdays when the Museum is closed.
