Our Basilica

To you, the people of all nations, a welcome to Mary's beloved Shrine - "this place of pilgrimage" long blessed by His Holiness Blessed John Paul the Great.

Ours is a house that faith built, these grounds now hallowed by the prayers and continuing sacrifices of Christ's faithful, yesterday, today and tomorrow.

The Basilica

Enter friend, enter pilgrim. For herein lies a House of God... a spiritual refuge for all who seek comfort, uplift and renewal in union with Mary and her Divine Son.

Lift up your eyes, your heart; come rest your soul in the presence of Our Lord. Come find His peace.

Holy Doors

"To focus on the door is to recall the responsibility of every believer to cross its threshold. To pass through that door means to confess that Jesus Christ is Lord; it is to strengthen faith in him in order to live the new life which he has given us. ."
Saint John Paul II

Bishop Noonan has designated our Basilica's center doors as the Holy Doors of the Diocese of Orlando.

Mother & Child Outdoor Chapel

Years before the doors
would open on the church
at Mary's Shrine, a unique
outdoor chapel took form.
Within, a bronze sculpture
depicted the boy Jesus
rushing to the safe harbor
of His mother's arms.
As strikingly original as
the surrounding chapel,
it endures, the very image
of maternal love.
