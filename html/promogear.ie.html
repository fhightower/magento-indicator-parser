Why Choose Promogear?

Promogear is an Irish owned company offering our clients the opportunity to order promotional merchandise online and delivered directly to their door! Our dedicated team of marketing professionals have a combined experience of 35 years in the promotional products world, and our clients range from major international companies to SME’s. Our clients are our priority, with customer service being always at the forefront of our minds. Promogear is about making life easy! But we are available to help our clients through the process when needed.

We work with key suppliers worldwide, offering client’s quality, innovative and cost effective solutions. We understand that promotional products are used to build your brand profile, therefore, we bring together a selection of well chosen products for our clients to select from. Whether it is a trade show, conference, sponsorship, exhibition or launch, we have a product to suit your needs and budget!

Our website features a selection of products to suit all budgets, our best selling products show clients what has worked for others so that they can learn from this and our trade show bundles take all the work out of selecting a variety of products. We update these products based on market trends, and are constantly observing, learning and researching what makes for a memorable promotional product.

So place your order today with Promogear, and find out just how easy ordering promotional merchandise can be. We are on hand should you need any help navigating your way through the site!
