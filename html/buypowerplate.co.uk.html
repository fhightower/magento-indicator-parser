Welcome to buypowerplate.co.uk

Welcome to the official UK online retailer for Power Plate products®.

As the global leader in whole body vibration technology, Power Plate redefines optimal health and wellness equipment for every age and ability. Power Plate equipment can be found in health clubs, gyms, spas, wellness centres, hospitals, elite training facilities and in private homes.

Engineered precisely to apply the science behind the body’s natural response to destabilization, the Power Plate platform uses a consistent, safe and controlled level of vibration that moves in three directions (up and down, front and back, and side to side).

Each Power Plate model is certified as a medical device under the Medical Devices Directive (MDD), in addition to earning accreditation from the Inclusive Fitness Initiative (IFI). This indicates each model is manufactured to the highest European Health and Safety Standards and can be used by individuals with a wide range of physical abilities. In this way, Power Plate can be prescribed as part of a wider medical treatment program to assist with a variety of ailments and conditions.

View the range to find out which Power Plate is right for you. All Power Plate machines come direct from the manufacturer, ensuring each purchase will be the world-class product you expect.
