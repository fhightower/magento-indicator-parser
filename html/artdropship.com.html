  • Warning - 20th Century -by Mircho Koleshev

    Oil On Linen Canvas, Diptych

    The picture was created in a period of about 11 years as Mircho always would leave for revision. Thematically, it is divided into two parts - war and peace. Mircho used a style of iconography by transferring the Byzantine icon in the modern world. He also used the stylish features of realism, impressionism, abstractionism and surrealism. Symbols, texts, reliefs, signs, shapes and colors Mircho wanted to provoke people into thinking about these events, make them aware and to never lead to such dramatic and detrimental to humanity such things as death and destruction.
