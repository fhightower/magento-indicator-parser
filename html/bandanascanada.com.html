Where do you ship from?
All of our bandanas ship from our Toronto, Canada warehouse. Before shipping we inspect them to make sure they are not discolored or damaged in any way, to ensure you are getting the best quality.

How much is Shipping?
We ship all orders by FedEx ground so that it reaches you fast. It usually takes about 2-3 business days but  it will vary based on how far or remote you are from us. If you are located near the warehouse or will be in the GTA, you are able to pick up your order.

No registration required to get a shipping quote from the shopping cart.
