How It Works

Choose your recipes

You can choose from the recipes created by our culinary team or let us choose the ones we think you’ll enjoy.

We chop & deliver

We source the highest-quality ingredients and do all of the prepping for you. Once we’ve diced, chopped, and portioned, we deliver to your door in our climate-controlled vessel.

Enjoy in 30 minutes or less

Our step-by-step recipes and prep work mean that dinner will be on the table in 30 minutes or less so that you have more time for what’s important.
