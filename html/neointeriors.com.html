Interior Design Service Ordering

Providing our customers with the best interior design service is a priority at NEO Interiors.This is accomplished by spending quality time understanding the lifestyles of our customers and their homes. It is vital to see a space in order to fully comprehend what it needs, by supplying your name, contact number and address we will schedule an in home consultation at your convenience.

You will never be subjected to a universal template that can be obtained by anyone with Internet access. Instead, we will assign one of our specialists, who will come to you for your initial consultation of only $250 and will work directly with you from the inception of this exciting venture until the project is complete. The advantage is that you will not be inconvenienced by having to come to us, as our NEO Interiors associate will schedule an on-site visit with you based upon your availability and will be able to fully analyze the condition and structural integrity of the existing space, assess the integration of the rest of your home, obtain accurate dimensions, offer professional suggestions, brainstorm with you regarding your desired layout, furnishings, and ornamentation, and will provide estimated costs based upon your budgetary needs.

Because we recognize the significance of such a substantial investment into the development of your living space, we will apply your paid consultation fee to your purchase amount of over $2500 to ensure that you are maximizing the value of this momentous project.

We are pleased to offer our expertise in interior design and guidance to help create your dream home.

Please fill out the details of your order below, and we will call you to schedule an appointment and discuss initial steps of your project.
