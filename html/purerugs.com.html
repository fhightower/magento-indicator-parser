Patchwork Cowhide Rugs

Designed by architects and crafted by artisans, Pure Patchwork Cowhide Rugs are fabricated with 100% natural material. They are geometric and dynamic in their design to elevate your senses. We start with a selection of the best cowhides from the Pampas Grasslands in Argentina and Brazil. Then each Patchwork Cowhide Rug is individually hand crafted to the scale of the pattern and assembled with a high resistant cotton ribbon. The rugs are finished with a perfect zig-zag stitching detail. All Pure cowhides are natural, not dyed or glued to any backing material. The reverse side of the rug is the natural soft suede that adheres perfectly to your floor so there is no need for any rug pad underneath. The characteristics of cowhide combined with our quality makes these Patchwork rugs naturally stain repellant and extremely durable.
 

Natural Cowhides

Originating from the world-renowned Pampas Grasslands of Argentina & Brazil, Pure cowhides are individually selected for their superior quality, shine and softness. The Pure Cowhide rugs are not dyed as we only use the natural coloring of cowhide. These premium cowhides are hand finished for the perfect look. Naturally stain resistant, these rugs are very durable and easy to maintain. Collection: White Cowhide, Grey Freckles Cowhide, Caramel Cowhide, Tan Cowhide, Black and White Cowhide, Brown and White Cowhide, Normand Cowhide, Black Cowhide, Classic Brindle Cowhide, Wheat Cowhide, Exotic Zebu Cowhide, Tricolor Cowhide, Gold Cowhide, Exotic Grey Cowhide.
 
 

Sheepskin Rugs

There is nothing we like more than the softness of Pure Sheepskin and Shearling on our feet and body. These premium Patagonian Sheepskin Rugs are life enhancing items that are made with natural hides that are free from dyes. In conjunction with the 100% Patagonian Sheepskin and Shearling, our backing is a Natural Cotton Panama Canvas that will protect and sustain the longevity of your rug. Each rug is made to order with a plush pile ranging from 2-3" for Sheepskin and ¾" for Shearling. It does not get more comfortable yet luxurious.
