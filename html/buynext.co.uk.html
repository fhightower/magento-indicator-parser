    XENON HID BULBS AND CONVERSION KITS

    D1R, D1S, D2R, D2S, D3S and other D type direct replacement xenon HID bulbs are for vehicles with HID headlights fitted by manufacturer. These bulbs will fit new car and motorbike models that come with manufacturer installed High Intensity Discharge lighting systems.

    H1, H4, H7, H11 and other aftermarket H type bulbs and HID conversion kits are used to convert halogen headlights to HID lighting system and provide up to 300% more light on the road.
