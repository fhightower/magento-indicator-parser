Top-Quality Hand Tools, Power Tools, Spare Parts and Accessories


If you are looking for the best tools at the best prices, whether for DIY projects or large-scale commercial jobs, United Tools McKinnon should be your first port of call. Serving business and private customers in south eastern Melbourne, we have a wide range of power tools, hand tools and accessories in our store. All the leading brands are well represented and our knowledgeable staff will be more than happy to help you choose the right tool for the job, if you should need any assistance. You can shop by brand or product on our website, so it should be easy to find what you need.

Around the home

For the man or woman who likes to tackle repairs around the home, we have a great selection of attractively priced tools that are idea for such work. If you are looking for drills, Hitachi, Makita and Metabo all produce budget models for the price conscious handyman as well as fully featured power drills for the more ambitious DIY enthusiast. We also stock a great selection of angle grinders, belt sanders, saws and heat guns that are sure to come in handy when you are working on your next home improvement project.

Trade

If you are a professional tradesperson, you will be delighted with the range of high quality tools in our McKinnon store. Whether you are looking for a capable demolition hammer, rotary hammer drills that will cut through the hardest of surfaces or a variable-speed router that can make short work of commercial carpentry jobs, we have what you need. All of the high-end tools that we sell come with comprehensive manufacturers' warranties for your peace of mind and should give you many years of trouble-free service. We understand what it means to be a professional and are committed to providing a first-class service to all of our trade customers.

In the Garage

Whether you are an amateur or a professional, you will find that our selection of Ross Compressors are ideal for spray painting applications as well as for powering pneumatic ratchets, wrenches and other air tools that can make light work of the most demanding of auto repair jobs. The pneumatic nailers we carry can also be used with Ross air compressors, and can drive large nails into wood, mouldings or other hard surfaces with the greatest of ease. Let United Tools McKinnon help you to take the sweat out of hard labour.

Local Knowledge, National Backing

A locally-owned store that is part of a national chain gives you the best of both worlds. Our size enables us to offer some of the most competitive deals around and our local knowledge means that we understand the unique problems and challenges faced by DIY enthusiasts and tradespeople in this part of Melbourne. For all types of projects, we can provide you with the equipment that you need to get the job done quickly and efficiently. Call 03 9578 2022 today and find out what we can do for you.
