AGOD

AGOD also known as Aboriginal Gallery of Dreamings, is a Melbourne based leading aboriginal art gallery. It is an institution acclaimed for its dedication in housing quality and original aboriginal paintings.

The AGOD site provides access to a wide variety of aboriginal artwork from aboriginal communities all over Australia. This art works range from small and decorative pieces to top end masterpieces by world-renowned aboriginal artists such as Emily Kame Kngwarreye, Clifford Possum Tjapaltjarri, Barbara Weir and the four Pwerle Sisters among many other acclaimed Australian aboriginal artists. More than three thousand paintings are available for online purchase on this site.
