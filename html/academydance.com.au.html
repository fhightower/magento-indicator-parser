About Us
The Australian Academy of Dance opened its doors in 1989. Since then, we have been helping students of all ages grow and develop into dancers, teachers and graduates.

With disciplines in ballet, jazz, tap, hiphop, contemporary, musical theatre, adult fitness and dance professionals, The Academy caters to all ages and abilities.

Please explore our site to learn more about our programs and if you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

Come and see why The Academy is one of Melbourne’s premier dance schools.
