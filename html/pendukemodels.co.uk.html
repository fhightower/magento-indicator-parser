Welcome

 

PendukeModels

supply scenics and accessories suitable for all genres of modelling from model railways to warhammer gaming and architectural models.

From small dioramas to large scale model railways or gaming boards we are sure to have the scenery to make your models even more life like and special.

Our range of warhammer scenics is great for both classic and 40k variants, a wide range of scenery, props and accessories are available.
From flowering meadows to the rolling green hills of England, where ever your scene is set we have the right shade and texture of grasses and flocks to match.
Add even more realism to your scene with our wide range of coals, scatters and stone - in a variety of sizes colours and textures.
Natural scenics is one of the most difficult to perfect and our range of lichen, moss and hedgerows can help add that realistic effect to your layout or models.
A wide range of buildings and houses including accessories like turn-tables, bridges and roadways.
Ocassionally we have trains, carriages and wagons in stock at discounted prices, both new & pre-owned. Check here for regular deals and special offers.
