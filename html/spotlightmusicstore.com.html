About Us

Spotlight Music a fantastic family owned music store! We specialize in all types of guitars (acoustic, electric, bass, classical, travel, children’s, custom), but also have an amazing product assortment of amplifiers, ukuleles, banjos, mandolins, keyboards, band and orchestra instruments, percussion equipment, DJ lighting, and music instruction books/videos/songbooks.


We carry many brands including: Fender, Squire, Ibanez, Breedlove, Yamaha, Paul Reed Smith (PRS), Charvel, Jackson, Sabian, D’Addario, Snark, Oscar Schmidt, Deering, Godin, Gretsch, Ultimate Support, EVH, Shure, Kala, Toca, Hohner, MXR, Dunlop, Bugera, Behringer, Snail, Amahi, Roland, Boss, Latin Percussion, Washburn, Kyser, Audio Technica, Marshall, and many more.
