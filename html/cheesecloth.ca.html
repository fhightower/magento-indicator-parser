Cheesecloth Canada

Cheesecloth.ca carries a wide selection of cheesecloth grades, from Grade #10 cheesecloth to Grade #90 cheesecloth. We are a Canadian company, dedicated to providing top-notch customer service and fast turnaround times. Most orders ship out same day or next business day with FedEx ground (we have great shipping rates due to our large volume shipping discounts). This gives us the ability to get our cheesecloth out the door and on the way to our customers in a timely and cost-efficient manner, without sacrificing transit time. Our cheese cloth is Made in China, providing a consistent, high quality product. We offer cheesecloth both by the yard and by the box for more savings.
