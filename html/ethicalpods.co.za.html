Why is the Ethical Coffee Company special and what is a reusable Nespresso/Caffeluxe compatible pod?

 

Ethical Pods is an authorised South African distributor for the Coffee Duck ® refillable Nespresso coffee pods (compatible with Nespresso ® coffee machines and various other makes who's pods are the same size). We specialise in lowering the cost of enjoying coffee at your home or office. (We supply reusable Nespresso pods as well as fresh ground coffee). Save money on every cup of coffee and help look after the environment at the same time - our pods can be refilled and reused ... simply wash and refill. We also now offer red espresso which opens up the world of coffee-style beverages to those who don't drink coffee or are trying to avoid caffeine. Beyond wholesomely good red cappuccinos. macchiatos, lattes and Americanos, it also goes where coffee can't : into refreshingly different iced teas, juices and smoothies bursting with flavour and real goodness from the Earth. We also have a selection of filled pods from some of South Africa’s favourite coffee artisans.

About Ethical Pods
