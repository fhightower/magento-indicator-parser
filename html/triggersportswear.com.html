About Us

Trigger Sportswear.

Trigger Sportswear are a leading online supplier of personalised sportswear and kits in the UK.

At Trigger Sportswear we create bespoke, customised sportswear to your exact specifications. Whatever you’re looking for, you can be sure we have it. All of our shirts, shorts, jerseys and kits are made to the highest standard before shipping them to you in time for your big game. Our kit designer is the most flexible in the market. It allows you to design your own customised kit or garment online and purchase it there and then. All of our sportswear is produced quickly, but professionally. You can be assured that your item will be created to your exact requirements but it will be in your hands a lot sooner. Our aim is to provide quality sportswear that you will love, in industry leading times. We will deliver your customised product anywhere in the UK, in just 2-3 weeks. Orders for standard items can be delivered throughout the UK in just 2-3 days. Trigger Sportswear produce high quality, customised kits for a range of sports including cycling, football, netball and rugby at unbeatable prices. We want you to wear your kit with pride, whilst knowing it hasn’t broken your budget. Our sportswear is created to last. From the first game of the season until the last, you can be sure your garment is made to the highest standard, using only the best fabrics available. If you require any assistance or would like any information about our personalised kits, call us today on 0115 824 1125 or email hello@triggersportswear.com. Our friendly staff are always happy to help. Alternatively, you can contact us using the form on our contact us page and we will be happy to get back to you.
