Northern Ireland based supplier and retailer of Mobility Aids

Welcome to Mobility Northern Ireland, the place where you can find a wide selection of high quality mobility aids and equipment at discount prices. We are a renowned mobility supplier throughout Coleraine, Belfast and Northern Ireland, but we can deliver our products across Ireland and the UK. We also offer technician installed delivery, which means we can provide full installation or set-up and train customers in the safe use and maintenance of our products.

Bringing you the best mobility equipment at affordable prices, online.

We stock a wide variety of products specifically designed to offer a helping hand and assist our customers live their day to day life independently. There’s no need to suffer in silence, buying mobility equipment can help people carry out their daily tasks more easily. Our superb range of mobility aids includes mobility scooters, wheelchairs, stairlifts, access equipment and more. If you can’t find what you’re looking for online, please call today for more information on our complete product range.

Need help? Require installation? Call today for a free consultation.

Mobility aids are a necessary part of many people’s day to day life throughout Northern Ireland, Ireland and the rest of the UK. Our products can make a real difference and, with an easy online ordering process, low prices and comprehensive aftersales support, Mobility Northern Ireland is the online mobility supplier you should count on for all of your mobility and access needs.
