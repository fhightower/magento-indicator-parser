Welcome to the Earth Tools Online Store! After years of operating without an online ordering option, we have created this Store so that our customers can place orders for some of our products at their convenience (instead of having to call within our business hours). (IF YOU ARE LOOKING FOR OUR MAIN WEBSITE SHOWING WALK-BEHIND TRACTORS AND IMPLEMENTS, GO TO WWW.EARTHTOOLS.COM)

In this Online Store you will find the same selection of excellent-quality gardening tools that we are famous for, plus some common replacement parts for our walk-behind tractors, their engines, and implements.

PLEASE NOTE that the shipping cost calculator on this online store is set to calculate long-handled tools in particular (which require more expensive packaging & "oversize" fees from the delivering carrier), and therefore, for smaller items, the shipping cost may be calculated a bit high. SO -- if you create an order online of "smaller" items, and you think the shipping cost seems "on the high side", PLEASE call to place the order instead, and of course you will be charged the correct amount for shipping costs.
