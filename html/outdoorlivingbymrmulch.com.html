Return Policy 

At Outdoor Living by Mr. Mulch, you may return the new, unused, resalable non-special order items purchased on our website within 30 days of purchase. Contact our customer service by filling out the INFORMATION REQUEST FORM to get a return authorization number before sending any item(s) back. Notifying customer service of the items to be returned will significantly expedite your refund. Please note: Some item(s) ship directly from the manufacturer, so some returns will need to be sent back to a different address. Once we have provided a return authorization number, simply ship the item(s) to the return address provided.

 

Upon receipt of returned merchandise, we will credit the amount you paid for the product(s), minus our original shipping cost. This may be different than what we charged you, we offer free freight but we do have shipping costs sending the product to you.  If you return the product, we will adjust the return for our original shipping charges).. We will advise you of the amount of this shipping cost when the return authorization is provided. When returning or exchanging item(s) please keep the original packing. We are unable to send out a box or packaging for returns or exchanges. If you do not have the original packaging, you will be responsible for obtaining a new packaging and/or box. The shipping cost will be provided for you when you receive the return authorization.

 

Please note that item(s) will be inspected upon return to determine if the item(s) are in resalable condition. If upon inspection, the item(s) are deemed unsalable, your credit will be subject to approval. 

 

 

Special Order Cancellation Policy

Special orders are orders containing merchandise that is not held in regular stock and is ordered at the specific request of the customer. As such, special orders cannot be cancelled, returned or exchanged under any circumstances once Outdoor Living by Mr. Mulch has placed the order with the manufacturer or after 72 hours when the order is placed. Customer is required to pay the balance of the purchase price when order is placed or under special circumstances when special order items have arrived at our warehouse.  Once balance is paid in full we will deliver or ship the special order items or you can pick them up at our warehouse.  Customer's failure to pay full balance so shall not relieve Customer of its obliigation to pay the entire amount owned for such merchandise.

About Us

Outdoor Living is a full service design & installation company of outdoor living spaces as well as direct sales of grills, outdoor furniture and hearth products. Outdoor Living was founded by Craig Schweitzer, a Gulf War Veteran. Schweitzer and his family have owned and operated Mr. Mulch since 1995. He went on to open Outdoor Living in 2008. 

Our company takes pride in serving our customers, and we are continually striving to enhance our level of customer service. We want to ensure that you get the best experience possible from your initial consultation to the completion of your project. We are committed to to remain the leader in the landscape installation and outdoor living industry in Columbus, Ohio. Our dedication to be the best design and construction company will show when you end up with the best looking outdoor living space on your block!

Outdoor Living, along with the other Schweitzer companies, is actively committed to actively supporting Columbus and its surrounding communities. Since the 1990s, we have helped various organizations, churches and schools net over $1,000,000 for their causes. We donate money and products to these organizations: We step up to answer the community’s call whenever possible.
