Where do you ship from?
All of our lace trim and lace ribbon items ship out of our toronto warehouse. We inspect each length of lace that we cut, to make sure that the quality is up to par with our Canadian standards of quality.

How much is Shipping?
We ship all orders by FedEx ground so that it gets to you fast, generally 2-3 business days. If you are located close to the warehouse or are going to be in the Toronto area you are also able to pick up your order.
