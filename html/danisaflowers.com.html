Flowers are more than our business flowers are our passion.

Danisa's Wholesale Fresh Flowers, Inc is a reliable and customer friendly source of fresh, long lasting flowers in bulk for your florist shop, wedding planning business or wholesale wedding flowers. Danisa's offers bulk flowers direct from farms in South and Central America as well as from Holland and California. Our wholesale flowers suppliers include some of the best flower farms in Colombia, Ecuador, Chile, and Holland.

Danisa's is managed by a staff with a combined 25 years of experience in the flower industry as flower growers, technical consultants to flower growers, flower designers and as wholesale roses distributors.

Our experience in the wholesale wedding flowers business has given us the knowledge to select the best farms and the best varieties of wholesale flowers from each of our farms in order to ship to you the very finest selection of exotic flowers in bulk available. Our experience growing flowers and our agricultural background has made it a priority of ours to select and work with environmentally friendly farms that operate with labor friendly policies toward their employees and their employee's families. Growing bulk flowers is a labor intensive endeavor and we very much appreciate the efforts of all those who work so hard in the fields so far away to grow these beautiful wholesale roses for our businesses.

Created eleven years ago in the Washington DC area, Danisa’s has provided wholesale wedding flowers for event planners, florists and flower shops in Washington DC Metropolitan area and within the continental U.S market with a supply of fresh, high quality flowers in bulk since we opened our doors. As our reputation for excellence has grown our business customer base has also expanded. Many of the business, event planners and shops we supply prefer our quality and service when it comes to buy wholesale flowers for weddings and bulk flowers.

Danisa's takes care to make sure we are able to deliver the very best quality wholesale wedding flowers to every customer. We are personally involved in every aspect of the business from selecting the farm growing the flowers, to the oversight of the careful packing and shipping of the flowers from the farm to our facilities, to the choice of the artisans who combine and match our flowers into spectacular flower arrangements.

If flowers are your passion, if you want to provide your customers with a wide selection of fresh, quality wholesale roses, then we have a shared goal and interest. Check out our selection of wholesale flowers. If you like what you see we would love for you to join our flower network.
