Quality Garden Tools

 

We've been making quality garden tools since 1780.

Made by English craftsmen our forged garden spades and forks have heads made from a single piece of steel. This is then securely riveted to an ash shaft, the end of which has been steamed to form a "D" shaped handle. These tools are exceptionally strong and are backed by our lifetime guarantee. In addition, we also offer a selection of handy and unique tools such as our Dutch Hoe and rubber-tipped Wizard rake. 

The tools on this site are stocked in our California warehouse for fast delivery all across the US. Customer service is provided from our offices in San Francisco, as well as in England, to ensure personal service and support to our customers.
