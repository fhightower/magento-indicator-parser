Welcome to John Giles Services

John Giles Services is a small Engineering and Repair Company established in 2001, we are a part of Giles Engineering (established 1974) and Essex Steam (established 2003). 

We are General Engineers, Fabricators and Mechanical Engineers, Marine, Steam, Agricultural and Classic Vehicle Repair Engineers. We able to carry out sheet fabricating, Welding MMA, TIG, MIG, Site Mobile welding, Manual Centre and capstan lathes work: Manual Milling Machine work: and with our 8 ft 75 ton Hydraulic press brake: 12 Ton Hare High speed hydraulic press: 35 Ton adjustable stoke power press: 2mtr x 4mm Guillotine: Treading Machine, Multi Iron Workers and Pipe bending for 6mm to 50mm diameter, we do a variety of punching, pressings and folding work.

We Manufacture Small components for Trailer manufacturing, Chicken and Heating industries. A few years ago we manufactured and installed a 50 ft clear span foot bridge over the Great Ouse in Huntingdonshire. We also Repair Coaches and Lorries Mainly Electrical Faults, but will do some mechanical repairs.
