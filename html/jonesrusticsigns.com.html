You might be surprised at the care and craft that goes into every artfully battered and carefully tattered sign we create. Pride in workmanship is a hallmark of the Jones’ Rustic Sign Company.

From its handcrafted pine board, to every homespun sentiment expressed, each Jones’ Rustic Sign is unique - a work of art that adds character and charm to any home, cabin, or office.

All Jones’ Rustic Signs begin with an original Lorna Jones design. Every pine board is carefully sanded, stained, and painted to emphasize the distinctive character of the wood. Next, it is hand-stenciled using special shading techniques and signed by the artist. Our signs are for inside display, for use on a covered porch. Lorna and Ken have put out the same Christmas sign out for 16 years on their covered porch and it still looks great.

HOWEVER---If you put a wood sign out in the weather it will last only about a year. Signs on crackle boards (our most popular board finish) will be destroyed if they get wet as the crackle medium is water based.

Finally, each completed sign is hand-framed and individually distressed by a skilled craftsman to give it that warm, weathered, and one-of-a-kind feel.
