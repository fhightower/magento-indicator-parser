Silver Magpie offers a collection of enduring, stylish and well priced presents for people looking for gifts for the children in their lives or for other adults, on regular occasions such as birthdays and Christmas.

The 10 collections of nearly 200 gifts are largely British sterling silver pieces and are grouped by age, gender and use. Prices start at £15 and over half the gifts are under £50

For the child, there is a bit of extra fun for that ‘tear open the parcel’ moment – the rubber duck. We will send a different duck each time, to create a “collection within the collection”.

For the busy person, a Gift Registry & Email Reminder service is available so that you never forget that birthday again or what you have previously given. The pieces can be engraved, to make them even more special.

The gifts come in Silver Magpie pouches, wrapped in Boy or Girl Tissue paper and beautifully boxed and ribboned in the distinctive Silver Magpie colours.
