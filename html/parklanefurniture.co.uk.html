One of the UK's leading suppliers of classic, English, antique reproduction furniture finished in mahogany and yew.

 

We are one of the UK's major retailers of classical Reproduction Furniture. We sell classic English antique reproduction furniture in dark mahogany, mellow mahogany and yew. Our range includes mahogany dining tables and regency chairs, china cabinets, pedestal desks, bookcases, tv and video cabinets, yew and mahogany sideboards, occasional tables, coffee tables, yew dining tables and beautifully crafted chests of drawers.

All of our furniture is fully assembled, handmade and finished in England by experienced craftsmen with years of experience.

We deliver all across the UK and place your new furniture in the room of your choice.

So enjoy browsing and please call us for free if you would like to know more about our furniture.
