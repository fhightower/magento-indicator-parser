PROUDLY MADE IN MONTREAL, CANADA

It seemed like a great idea…Create beautiful, well fitting intimate apparel using quality fabrics, and do it with standout style.

With One 2 One, it became a reality in 1998.

Proudly made with love from our base and production facility in Montreal, Canada, one of the world’s hippest, most cosmopolitan cities, our dynamic design team keeps a finger on the pulse of what’s happening. With a long tradition in producing stylish intimate apparel, One 2 One is haute gamme in look and feel. Each garment is crafted from the finest fabrics and laces, purchased from the world’s most renowned mills.

We continue to pride ourselves on remaining dedicated to innovative design, comfort, fit, quality, and Canadian production.

We, at One 2 One, look forward to sharing our passion with you…After all, getting dressed or undressed should be fun!
