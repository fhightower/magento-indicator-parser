Our Shop

Preservation Station is the source for architectural antiques for designers, builders, and home-owners. We specialize in antique lighting and have an extensive selection of antique chandeliers and sconces. We carry a large array of antique hardware, mantels, stained glass windows, doors, and other architectural salvage. A very small percentage of our inventory is listed online -- if you are looking for something special, please call us!
