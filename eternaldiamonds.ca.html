One of Montreal's Premier Jewellery Stores

Eternal Diamonds is here to help you make the most of those special, fleeting moments in life that you will forever embody and passionately cherish. You most certainly have choices when choosing which jewellery stores you will shop at. Our goal is to offer you the finest selection of laboratory certified Canadian diamonds and designer jewellery, so that you can fondly remember the most important of life’s moments for many years to come.

Large Selection of Canadian Diamonds

We are pleased to offer one of the most robust and elegant, immaculate collections of Canadian diamonds available. We take the time to meticulously select every stone we feature by hand, grading them based upon the Four C’s: carat, clarity, color and cut. Only the most brilliant and gorgeous stones make it passed our stringent grading process. This is how we are able to offer the utmost in quality to our valued customers time and time again.

Popular Shapes & Cuts Available

As one of the leading diamond jewellery stores in Canada, we are pleased to offer a massive catalog that features more than 100,000 loose diamonds. We offer diamonds of nearly every shape, weight and cut available with plenty of options for customization. Shop for your favorite shapes and cuts today to create a custom ring that’s truly unforgettable and inimitably priceless.

Our Guarantee to Customers

Our assurance to our customers is simple and straightforward: your shopping experience will be unique and one-of-a-kind. You will find the quality diamonds and jewellery that you want with an unrivaled customer experience backed by our guarantee to all of our shoppers.

  • Free ring sizing for up to five years from purchase.
  • Free lifetime cleaning and inspection on all jewellery.
  • Free re-tipping of claws for a full decade from purchase.
  • Lifetime re-shaping on all rings we offer.

An Unforgettable Shopping Experience

As one of Canada's most respected jewellery stores, we are dedicated to delivering an unforgettable shopping experience. Our support staff is more than happy to provide the shopping advice and assistance that you need today with making your decision. We are available via email or telephone to aid you in your purchase and to help provide answers to any important questions you have regarding our inventory.
