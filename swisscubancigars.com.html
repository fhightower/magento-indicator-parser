Storing Cuban Cigars

Storing Cuban Cigars

Ideally, your cigars should come with a humidor to maintain its freshness and to enhance its flavor. This should never be a problem since we also have the best cigar humidors and accessories to give you the most pleasurable smoking experience. Humidors are must have items for cigar smokers since these do not only store the cigars that you purchase on a short term basis but also contribute much to its taste.

With such, you can enjoy our premium Cuban cigars more and they will be more aged and ready to puff so long as you crave for a lavish smoke.

The humidors cannot be used right away since you need to season it in order not to ruin your cigars. All that is needed is to dampen a cloth with water until it is soaked but not dripping with water. Wiping the humidors with the cloth will help maintain the humidity of the humidor. After which, placing the damp cloth over a plastic to ensure that it will not have a direct contact with the wood is the next step. Fill the humidification device with distilled water and ensure that no excess water or moisture on the device before placing it back into the humidor.

Humidors should be kept sitting for a whole day which let the cedar in the humidor retain moisture and age. The steps mentioned above should be repeated once again and once finished, your premium Cuban cigars can now be placed inside. It is highly important to maintain the right level of humidity when storing cigars in a humidor and the best humidity levels are around 68-72. When the humidity is out of range, you can add water so to normalize the humidity levels.

Cuban cigars can be enjoyed without aging them in the humidor but if you wish for more intense flavor then you can allow your cigars to age for at least one to two months. We do not only provide you with the best grade cigars but you will also find a lot of information on storage and aging your cigars along with our other cigar accessories and storage products.
