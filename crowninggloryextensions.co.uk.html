Big Voluminous hair has never been so popular thanks to the likes of celebrities such as Cheryl Cole, Tulisa, Amy Childs and many more. Want to create that look without pulling on the purse strings? Well now you can by using crowning glory hair extensions.

Unlike many other companies out there who offer standard clip in sets made up of 10 pieces that total around 100g of hair our crowning glory quad weft clip in sets are made up of just 7 pieces and total a whopping 230g of hair. Gone are the days of trying to squeeze every last clip onto your hair without them been visible and still ending up with thin hair. Quad weft sets are most defiantly a girls new best friend. We also stock our delux DIY weft that contains a huge 160grams of hair and our best selling 200 gram super weft.  All our clip in hair extension sets come with aftercare guides and instructions on how to wash and care for your new treasured locks.
