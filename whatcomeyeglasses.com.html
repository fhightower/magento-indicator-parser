Whatcom eyeglasses is an extension of our brick and mortar clinics spanning Washington State and British Columbia, Canada.  Our goal is to provide you, the consumer, with great eyeglass selections and great quality lenses at affordable prices. All of our lenses include a scratch resistant coating, eyeglass case, cleaning cloth, and single vision lenses; all for one low fee! The frames and lenses are backed by our brick and mortar clinic locations, and are all manufactured in the state of Washington using Zeiss quality lenses. We have Low Prices and only sell 100% authentic frames; guaranteed.
