Not all candles are created equally

We love candles and hope that you do too. However, some candles can cause harm, especially those imported cheaply from China and the Far East. Paraffin and lead are two items which you should be aware of and try to avoid when buying candles.

Paraffin is the major ingredient found in most conventional candles. Paraffin is a sludge waste product from the petroleum industry. It releases eleven carcinogenic toxins when burned, including cancer-causing benzene and toluene which affects the central nervous system. The soot which is released into the environment is similar to that released from a diesel engine and can be as dangerous as second-hand cigarette smoke, contributing to serious respiratory issues like asthma

Lead is used in some wicks to stiffen them, particularly in candles manufactured in China, where there is a lack of regulation in the market. Candles with a lead-core wick release five times the amount of lead considered hazardous for children and exceeds pollution standards for outdoor air. Exposure to high amounts of lead has been linked to hormone disruption, behavioural problems, learning disabilities and numerous health problems


We use 100% natural pure EcoSoya® soy wax

Soy wax is a natural product made from soybeans which are vegetables. Being a natural product it is biodegradable and cleans up with soap and water

Compared to paraffin, it burns cooler, lasts 50% longer and throws a stronger scent due to a lower melting point and a larger wax pool

Most importantly, it releases no known carcinogens or toxins
