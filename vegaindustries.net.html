Superior Quality Industrial Fastener Tools

For over 25 years Vega has been producing industry leading driver bits and other fastener tools. The benefit of Vega tools is improved productivity of assembly operations and construction professionals. This is accomplished with the most proven durable fastener driving tools on the market and a relentless focus on quality.

All of our tools are manufactured and designed in our company owned ISO 9001:2008 facility. We do not source or contract out production to other manufacturers. This gives us complete control in design and quality. The materials and manufacturing processes used in the production of our fastener tools are regarded to be some of the best in the world today. Reduced cam-out and extended service life are the benefits consistently experienced by our end users.
