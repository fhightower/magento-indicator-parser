TILE DART

Welcome to Tile Dart. Located in Miami, FL, we are a decades old tile retailer that offers one of the broadest line of arabescato marble tile, travertines and glass mosaics in the market. We are now proud to present to you our collection online. When designing an interior, the choice of flooring is what sets the tone for the rest of the project. Flooring materials are also the most difficult to change. Therefore one must make extensive research and be sure about the character they want their flooring to bring out. Tiles are one of the longest lasting options for flooring. One type that has been receiving interest is the ceramic tile and chinese honey onyx. Easy to maintain and with endless varieties, there is a ceramic tile for every taste. Tile Dart carries an extensive range of tile options from wood looking ceramic tiles to natural travertine tiles.
