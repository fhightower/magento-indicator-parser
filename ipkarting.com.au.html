About IPK

IPKarting is a leading kartsport manufacturing company based in Verona, Italy. Formerly the home of the Intrepid Kart Technology brand and SKM range of parts, IPKarting is now a dominant force in global karting with their brands Praga, OK1 and IPK precision spare parts range.
