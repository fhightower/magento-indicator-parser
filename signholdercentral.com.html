Sign Holder Central is a competitive acrylic sign holder manufacturer. In-house manufacturing grants us huge gains in quality control, cost savings and selection. We in turn, can offer you the following money-saving incentives: Thick High Quality Acrylic, Competitive Pricing, Volume Price Breaks, Quick Turn Around Times and No Broken Case Fees or Minimum Order Requirements. Yes, you read that correctly. You may order just one sign holder or photo frame, or a dozen without incurring a fee or extra handling charges.
