Seed Starting Advice

Getting a head start on a growing season is an absolutely wonderful way to harvest earlier and harvest more per growing season all you need is a seedling tray and some growing lights. Or you could stick the seedling tray filled with rich soil and your choice of seed by a well lit window. Don't forget to keep the sprouts nice and moist. When its time to transplant your seedlings some plants like tomatoes need to be acclimated. For about a week give the sprouts two to three hours of outside time a day to get used to conditions of wind and sun.
