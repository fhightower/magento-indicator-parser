Why I need to do the course,
“Plunder Hell to Populate Heaven”

If we are not “fired-up”, we cannot convey God’s nature to others. Therefore, everything in this book is about the Bible God of fire, who fire-baptizes us with his Spirit and sends us to preach the good news to all creation.

Whatever God tells us he is, that is what we need to know. Whoever is called to be a compassionate soul-winner or evangelist needs to know God’s temperament of zeal, fire and love. Anyone who is saved is called to save souls! 

When you complete this course you will receive a signed certificate

What is in the course
and how much will it cost?

Created as profound theological teaching material, this unique study book will ignite every Christian to become an effective soul-winner and passionate fisher of men.

This School of Fire® is a self paced E Learning course and provides Christians worldwide with excellent teaching on individual and corporate evangelism. It will turn the call, the desire, and the passion for soul-winning into effective action.
