 The J.M. Brady Company is a third generation family business, distributing canine and feline hard goods and treats. Founded in 1952 by Joseph M. Brady, the company is proudly run by his grandsons, Kevin and Brian Brady.

  Whether you've been a customer for years, or are trying us for the first time, we pledge to you that you'll receive the finest service in the industry. In addition, our expertise allows us to provide services as an importer, jobber, and order fulfillment provider, for select customers.

  You are welcome to visit us in our showroom, located 15 miles west of Boston, in Needham, Massachusetts. We offer mail order service nationwide, via UPS, and internationally, through the United States Postal Service. Our sales force can be at your door to personally handle your needs throughout New England. We also offer weekly delivery to most of New England with our own fleet of trucks.

  Try us and you'll find out how our personal attention to service will help your business grow.
