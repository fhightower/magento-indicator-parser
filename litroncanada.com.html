Welcome to Litron

     Litron Distributors Ltd. is a premier Canadian distributor for all your lighting requirements. Our renowned team of lighting experts have over 50 years of experience in the industry. Prior to starting Litron in 2009, the principals of the Company were the founding owners of the largest lighting distributor in North American. Whether you require retail, residential, commercial or industrial lighting we have the fixtures and lamps to suit all your needs. In 2011, 2012 and 2013, Litron (Montreal) was Hydro Quebec’s number 1 energy saving partner helping our customers save over 14 GWh (which is 14 billion watts of electricity).

     Litron has built its business on three basic principles: Quality, Price and Customer Service. Litron carries over 250 manufacturers of lighting products and accessories. We stand behind our products and will only distribute to you manufacturers that we trust. Our long standing relationships, coupled with our volumes allow Litron to deliver the best prices in Canada. We work with our vendors to keep our costs low and pass on those savings to you.

     We strive for happy, satisfied customers. With branches in Montreal, Toronto, Ottawa, Halifax and Winnipeg, and with our dedicated e-business team and support staff at support@litroncanada.com we are ready to answer all your lighting questions and service your lighting needs. When your other suppliers have left you the dark, let Litron show you the light and how we can help you save money on all your lighting needs.
