who we are - the short and sweet version

As well as shopping our full product selection on our online store, you can find Beauty Xpressions stores located in some of the UK's largest cities including Manchester, Glasgow and Liverpool. Our team of hair extension specialists can fit popular extensions such as Braided Weave, LA Weaves, Pre Bonded Extensions and Micro Ring Hair Extensions - allowing you to transform your hair with length and volume, all in the expert hands of our experienced team.
