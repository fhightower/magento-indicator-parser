What is ARMEASE?

It is a versatile woman’s fashion garment that “sleeves” short sleeve and sleeveless outfits when worn under outfit.  It can also be worn over any sleeve length as a short crop jacket, a shrug, or a choli. ARMEASE® has long sleeves with a secure center front snap at the lower bra line creating a deep V-neck. Solid fabric styles are made of a 4 way stretch, rayon derived from bamboo. Made in USA.

 

We now have five different styles of ARMEASE®.

Solid Black, Solid Creamy White, Solid Navy Blue, Limited Edition Black Lace, Limited Edition White Lace.
