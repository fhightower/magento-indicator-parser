RONIN RIGS

RoninRigs found its beginnings as a Beta tester for theDJI Ronin. I saw far greater potential for the Ronin than just shooting hand held. Steadicam , cablecam, car , crane, c-stand, crankovator, aerial platform or RC Car were all posibilities. "Necessity is the mother of invention" I required adapters to fully test the Ronin so i called our machinist who has over a decade of building high end camera and SPFX prototypes in Hollywood.

The Ronin was designed to handle a Red Dragon Camera and the RoninRigs Modular system was designed after the Reds modular system. Roninrigs adapters are compact, modular, and versatile.
