Read More: Shop for Plus Size Swimwear and Plus Size Swimsuits 

When it comes time for a great getaway or little fun in the sun closer to home, you'll find a great selection of trendy plus size bathing suits and cover-ups in gorgeous prints and colors. Shop your favorite designer swim brands and styles and find a great suit for all of your warm weather adventures.

Plus Size Swimwear-Find a generous selection of quality swimsuits for all, perfect for a day lounging by the pool, surfing the high seas, or relaxing with family and friends. Feel beautiful and confident in trendy plus size swimsuits that complement your body's curves. Discover innovate designs made with figure-friendly details like comfortable bust support, all over ruching and slimming tummy control panels. Shop by style, brand, color and fit to find the cute plus size swimwear of your dreams. You are unique and your swimsuit should be too. Whether you love the coverage of a one piece swimsuit or swimdress or the convenience of a tankini or bikini, when it comes to cute plus size swimwear, the possibilities are endless.

Plus Size Cover-ups - No cute plus size swimwear ensemble would be complete without a stunning cover-up. Discover an impressive collection of dresses, pareos and sarongs, skirts, pants and tunic tops. Play up your style with a trendy maxi dress that is as perfect for a seaside stroll as it is for dinner with friends. Shelter your skin from the sun in an adorable print tunic or wrap yourself in the luxury of a colorful pareo or sarong.

Plus Size Active Wear - Workout clothing takes on a new edge with this collection of street worthy activewear styles. Exercise your body and style with fitness wear that expresses a sense of style while you workout. Look and feel your personal best in workout pants and tops made from moisture wicking fabric—perfect for working up a sweat. Workout clothes can be fashion forward too. In an array of colors and prints these pieces will mix comfortably and easily with your girl on the go wardrobe.

Plus Size Lingerie - After a long day, everyone wants to slip into something special and comfortable. Enjoy sweet dreams in a soft cotton nightgown, flannel pajamas or rayon stretch knit sleepwear. Cuddle up on cold nights or after a shower in an ultra-soft fleece robe or jacket.
