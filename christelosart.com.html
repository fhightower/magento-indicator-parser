About Christelo's Art

Christelo's Art was started in the
summer of 2007 by husband and wife
team Angelo and Christine McKenzie.

Christine, having been a hobby artist for
a number of years, did not manage to
retain any of her work, because as soon
as they were finished and shown to
friends, they had a habit of
disappearing out the door.
