About Soulindha

Beautiful Hand Crafted Leather Bags - using tribal inspired fabrics, for a gypsyesque, bohemian world.

This is a place for you to come and see my designs.   Unique bags, all individually made, handcrafted to the finest quality.   The designs are inspired by communities around the globe.  Where the use of many tribal fabrics can be found in the bags.    Each bag, combines elegant chic, with bohemian, gypseque styles.  Colorful, and eyecatching, the bags are often described as pieces of art. 

Made with love, just for you, 'because you are magnificent'.
