Features & Benefits

Quality ingredients makes healthier dogs

5th Avenue Dog is the exclusive online distributor of My Perfect Pet fresh dog food, using only the highest quality human-grade ingredients made in the USA, prepared to the strictest FDA safety standards. With 5th Avenue Dog, you and your pet will love the safety and convenience that fresh food provides!

We view our dogs as family and we believe in feeding them what they deserve; we wouldn't give them food we weren't willing to eat ourselves. Our mission is to provide you and your pet with high quality, balanced, human grade food, so you can rest assured knowing exactly what goes into it and where it's coming from. We believe you should have the happiest, healthiest and longest time with your dog as you can.
