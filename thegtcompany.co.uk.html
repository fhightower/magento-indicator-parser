Welcome to The Garden Timber Company, based in the South East of England we manufacture high quality Wooden Products including Wooden Ponds, Planters and Sandpits. Constructed from modular 27mm or 44mm high quality timber each section slots together and with its tongue and groove top and bottom creates a strong wooden structure that is built to last.

All our timber is pressure treated, the treatment is ACQ and play park friendly and allows us to offer a 5 year guarantee on 27mm products and 10 year guarantee on 44mm products even if you chose to submerge the wooden structure in the ground.

That’s what makes our product so versatile, not only are they modular meaning there is a variety of different shapes, sizes and heights but they can be installed on concrete, patios, decking and grass. And if the garden isn’t level you can submerge part or all of the structure in the ground.
