DiscountDoorHardware News

Emtek offers one of the most diverse collections of door hardware available. Every piece has solid brass construction for reliability and strength, and the variety of available finishes adds timeless beauty to quality function. Emtek now carries a selection of deadbolts and electronic door locks to complement their existing product line and provide even more security choices. Emtek assembles each order individually as it's received, which gives customers the opportunity to choose door hardware that best suits their specific needs and tastes.

Schlage is easily one of the most respected names in door hardware. After more than 85 years of producing quality security products, it's no surprise that contractors and homeowners alike trust the Schlage name like no other. From front door to back and both upstairs and down, Schlage's affordable, innovative door hardware covers all your home needs. There are more than twenty front-door handlesets alone, and each comes with a matching interior knob or lever. We've distributed durable Schlage products since 1988, and we're proud to stock their entire product line.

Baldwin door hardware is composed of solid brass and is available in a wonderful variety of styles and finishes. Known for its exceptional quality, the Baldwin product line features designs to suit both contemporary and rustic tastes as well as everything in between. As one of the earliest manufacturers of door hardware, Baldwin has never stopped innovating. Their selection of keyless locks allows customers to enjoy security and convenience through the use of personal entry codes. The beauty of Baldwin door hardware also brings peace of mind.

Dynasty offers door hardware of exceptional quality and beauty. The solid construction features Schlage's 5-pin compatible keyways, and all Dynasty hardware comes with a 10-year mechanical warranty. In addition to the safety and durability Dynasty Hardware products provide, the range of eye-catching designs and attractive finishes will entice any homeowner. It's easy to create coordinated looks and satisfy every home need with Dynasty door hardware. We have the best prices anywhere for all Dynasty Hardware products; visit us often to take advantage of our current specials.

Commercial door hardware has been our specialty since 1988, and we stock one of the most extensive selections in the country. You'll find many major brands readily available here, including Falcon, Schlage, LCN, Cal-Royal, Von Duprin and Norton. The experts at DoorHardware.com will ensure that you always receive the best price available and that your orders arrive on time, every time. You can trust us with all your door hardware needs.
