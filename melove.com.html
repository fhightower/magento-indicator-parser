melove

 

At me love, we don't just serve raw fish, we also have an equally extensive selection of rice dishes with a range of toppings, traditional noodle dishes, skewers and many vegetarian food options. Everything is prepared fresh by our experienced sushi chefs, including the fish, which is fresh daily. We still hand roll and create all of the dishes using only the finest ingredients where possible.

We only prepare food once an order has been placed to ensure the best quality and we are proud to support sustainable fish suppliers and continually strive to reduce our environmental impact by introducing more recyclable and biodegradable packaging options.

For catering, dine in or delivery we offer the freshest food direct to your door. Ultimately, we want you to love sushi as much as me love!
