Why CPS?

Knowledge

With more than a decade of research and development in the polished concrete industry, CPS is committed to sharing its vast knowledge of the craft with others through our professional training sessions held at several locations throughout the year.

Experience

The founders of CPS were instrumental in the development of the polished concrete industry and have over a decade of experience in developing new polished concrete equipment, diamond tooling, chemicals and more!

Commitment

Our commitment to making life-long relationships is more than just a mission statement – it’s our way of doing business. At CPS, we give you the tools you need to be successful and we support you every step of the way!
