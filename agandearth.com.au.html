Welcome to Ag & Earth Hire

Ag & Earth Hire, an affiliate company of Agriquip Machinery Co. is an Agricultural & Construction equipment hire specialist, a single source for all your farm, construction & commercial machinery needs with both dry hire and wet hire options.

It’s not always practical or cost effective to buy your own farming, construction or commercial equipment, that’s why we are here. As providers of Agricultural, Construction & Commercial equipment hire, we cater to all your heavy and light equipment and machinery needs. 

Our range of equipment includes includes compact and utility tractors, large ag tractors, skid steers & excavators, telehandlers and a large assortment of tractor attachments and farming equipment including drills, ploughs, mulches and slashers. We also stock a range of outdoor power equipment, ride-on lawn mowers.

Our rates are extremely competitive and much cheaper than owning your own gear especially for seasonal events likes seeding, ploughing or mowing.

We hire out equipment on a daily or long-term basis. All our equipment is manufactured to high-quality standards, from well-known brands that you can rely on for strength, durability and flexibility.

If you are looking for quality machinery and a company with knowledgeable and friendly staff, that provides the most reliable advice and exceptional service, then Contact us to discuss your hire requirements. We will assess your needs and provide you with practical, workable solutions that are cost-effective and suitable to your needs.

Whether you’re a contractor, farmer, builder, landscaper or you run a mine, our team of machinery hire specialists can help you choose the best equipment to get the job done right, on time and within budget.
