About Pino

Founded in Germany in 1904, PINO has developed products and therapy concepts used by spas throughout Europe.

PINO Natural Spa Therapy is dedicated to a greater harmony with body, mind and spirit. The PINO formulations use carefully selected plant extracts and essential oils.
