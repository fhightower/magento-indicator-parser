Meet Janice Kehoe, Owner of Solo Antiques

I am a specialist antiques dealer in Art Nouveau and Art Deco Sculpture and Glass (mainly European) as well as Arts and Crafts (English & Scottish) of the period 1880 to 1935. Whether you are looking for a piece from specific artists like Galle, Daum, Loetz, Moser, and Rene Lalique or Sculpture from Chiparus, Preiss or Lorenzl or Arts and Crafts from Newlyn, Liberty or Keswick.

We also offer a search and find service if you are looking for a specific piece.
