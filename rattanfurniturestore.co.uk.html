About Us

Garden and Patio furniture has always been a popular product, but can sometimes be associated with the work required to maintain it. Wooden furniture sets can deteriorate over time, and metal sets will rust if not looked after. The reason that rattan furniture has become so popular is simple. It is low maintenance, lightweight and sturdy. What more could you ask for?

Free Delivery on Everything

At Rattan Furniture Store we understand how frustrating it can be to have almost finished shopping and comparing online, only to find that the website you decided to go to throws a fifty pound delivery charge on top. We offer completely free delivery on all of our products, so rest assured that the price you see on our product page is inclusive of everything you need!

Furniture Accessories

We know that shopping online is all about ease, and we want to supply you everything you need in one place. So this is why we have a wide range of parasols, cushions, covers and heaters - so that you can shop online and get it done in one go.
