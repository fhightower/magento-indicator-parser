About Gatsbylady London "I started sewing and designing clothes since I was a little girl. Started working in factories. Learned every aspect of sewing and pattern making. After that, I strengthen my knowledge with a postgraduate degree from University of London and MSc degree in Fashion from one of the leading University in Great Britain.”

"After my handful degrees and production background I moved on working within the high fashion designer industry for many years. I had dreamed of setting up my own business and designing the way I feeI each day one day. What sets Gatsbylady apart is the quality of our fabrics, our unique hand embellished designs and the flexibility of our fits. Our designs are individual, effortless, comfortable and stretches by a size for an easy fit. Gatsbylady dresses make you feel special and beautiful"
