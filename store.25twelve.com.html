        • Free Delivery/Shipping on All Orders 

          25Twelve which is a Amsterdam based company delivers and ships to any place in the world, free of charge to make it as easy and simple for our Customers.

Once we have received the returned products, the products will be inspected and the process of refunding will be processed within 14 days.

Once our customer service team has received all the necessary information and details, they can start answering your question or problem in a timely and accurate manner.
