Welcome to Desser Furniture

Thinking of buying new Rattan, Cane, Wicker or Bamboo Furniture for your Conservatory, Sun Room or any other room in your house at great prices?

Desser Cane Furniture has a 12 month guarantee on all frames, comes with Pirelli webbing for extra support & strength & all 2016 suites feature Dacron wrapped base cushions for shape retention and softness.

Here you can choose Rattan and Wicker Furniture that is fashionable, functional, durable and Eco-Friendly.

Rattan furniture is extremely versatile for your home, hotel, restaurant, lobbies, poolside and sun rooms.

Wicker Rattan furniture is durable for all indoor settings, offices, luxury suites, common areas, dining, living rooms, conservatories settings and much much more.

How about our new outdoor dining sets, garden furniture, patio sets or even corner sofas, if you're thinking of entertaining outside this summer.

If you have any questions regarding any of the products you find on this site please contact us on 0845 6444195.
