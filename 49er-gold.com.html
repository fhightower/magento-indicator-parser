About Us

Established in 1985 in the heart of Gold Country, nestled at the foothills of the Sierra Nevada mountain range, our brick-and-mortar store lies tucked away for the prospectors at heart and miners who practice full-time. We have served our community for decades, and have since expanded our presence to encompass the global aficionados in the prospecting arts. Our work endears us to the same level of commitment and values for many years to come.
