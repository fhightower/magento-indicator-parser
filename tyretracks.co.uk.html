ALCOA Wheels

Tyretracks are a Premium Distributor for the world famous ALCOA wheels. Tyretracks are the UK’s leading Alcoa wheels specialist in addition to supplying other truck accessory products and services. We're a long established company that is family owned and run - you can count on us for a reliable and efficient service.

Alcoa wheels really are the leading wheels on the market today, they are the lightest and the strongest wheel on offer today. We keep a large stock of new & second hand Alcoa wheels that are ready to be despatched for next day delivery using one of our trusted carriers.

ALCOA Truck Wheels - Aluminium Truck Wheels

One of Alcoa's most recognised products are the forged aluminium truck wheels, as well as aluminium wheels for trailers and buses. Alcoa wheels offer many advantages compared to steel wheels including being lighter, stronger, more environmentally friendly, easier to clean and nicer looking! Learn more about the key advantages of Alcoa aluminium truck wheels. 

Dura-Bright Wheels - Dura-Bright EVO Wheels®

Alcoa's Dura-Bright wheels do not need polishing - helping you save money and working hours! Dura-Bright EVO wheels do not chip, crack, peel or corrode, like conventional coatings on other aluminium or steel wheels do. The Dura-Bright treatment is available on most Alcoa wheel sizes. For further details, please consult the Alcoa Wheels Specification sheet. We hope you enjoy exploring our website. Please feel free to contact us and speak to the team.
